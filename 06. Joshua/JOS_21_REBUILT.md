## CHAPTER SUMMARY

Levitical heads approach Eleazar, Joshua, and tribal heads at Shiloh, requesting cities to dwell in with suburbs for cattle as commanded by Moses. Israel gives Levites cities and suburbs by lot. Kohathites: Aaron's priestly family receives 13 cities from Judah, Simeon, and Benjamin. Remaining Kohathites receive 10 cities from Ephraim, Dan, and half-Manasseh. Gershonites receive 13 cities from Issachar, Asher, Naphtali, and half-Manasseh in Bashan. Merarites receive 12 cities from Reuben, Gad, and Zebulun. Aaron's priests receive Hebron (city of refuge), Libnah, Jattir, Eshtemoa, Holon, Debir, Ain, Juttah, Beth-shemesh (9 cities from Judah and Simeon), plus Gibeon, Geba, Anathoth, Almon (4 cities from Benjamin) - total 13 cities. Remaining Kohathites receive Shechem (city of refuge), Gezer, Kibzaim, Beth-horon (4 cities from Ephraim), Eltekeh, Gibbethon, Aijalon, Gath-rimmon (4 cities from Dan), Tanach, Gath-rimmon (2 cities from half-Manasseh) - total 10 cities. Gershonites receive Golan (city of refuge), Beesh-terah (2 cities from half-Manasseh), Kishon, Dabareh, Jarmuth, En-gannim (4 cities from Issachar), Mishal, Abdon, Helkath, Rehob (4 cities from Asher), Kedesh (city of refuge), Hammoth-dor, Kartan (3 cities from Naphtali) - total 13 cities. Merarites receive Jokneam, Kartah, Dimnah, Nahalal (4 cities from Zebulun), Bezer, Jahazah, Kedemoth, Mephaath (4 cities from Reuben), Ramoth (city of refuge), Mahanaim, Heshbon, Jazer (4 cities from Gad) - total 12 cities. Total Levitical cities: 48 with suburbs. The LORD gave Israel all promised land, they possessed and dwelt there. God gave rest, delivered all enemies into their hand, and fulfilled every good thing spoken to Israel.

**Joshua 21:2-3** — "And they spake unto them at Shiloh in the land of Canaan, saying, The LORD commanded by the hand of Moses to give us cities to dwell in, with the suburbs thereof for our cattle"

- **Numbers 35:1-8** — "And the LORD spake unto Moses in the plains of Moab by Jordan near Jericho, saying, Command the children of Israel, that they give unto the Levites cities to dwell in... And the cities which ye shall give shall be of the possession of the children of Israel: from them that have many ye shall give many; but from them that have few ye shall give few: every one shall give of his cities unto the Levites according to his inheritance."
  - *Interpretation:* Numbers provides the original command for Levitical cities, which Joshua now fulfills, showing the proportionate distribution based on each tribe's inheritance size.

  *Interpretation:* Deuteronomy explains that while Levites have no territorial inheritance, God provides for them through cities and offerings, showing His care for those who serve Him full-time.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "There failed not ought of any good thing which the LORD had spoken"
**Common misinterpretation:** This means life was perfect
**Biblical context:** This refers specifically to God's covenant promises about the land being completely fulfilled. It doesn't mean there were no challenges, but that God's specific promises were kept.

### "The LORD gave them rest round about"
**Common misinterpretation:** This means no more conflict ever
**Biblical context:* This refers to the completion of the major conquest phase. Later books show conflicts continued, but the major military conquest was complete. This rest was the fulfillment of God's promise for that season.
