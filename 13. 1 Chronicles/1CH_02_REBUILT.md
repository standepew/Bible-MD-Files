## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### REUBEN
- **Meaning:** "Behold, a son"
- **Context:** Firstborn son of Jacob
- **Significance:** Tribe that lost birthright
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual firstborn (Romans 8:29)

### JESSE
- **Meaning:** "He will cause to forget"
- **Context:** Son of Jacob who sold birthright
- **Significance:** Tribe that received birthright
* **Spiritual application:** Spiritual inheritance (1 Peter 1:4)

### LEVI
- **Meaning:** "Joined" or "attached"
- **Context:** Tribe of priests
- **Significance:** Set apart for God's service
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual priesthood (Hebrews 7:5)

### DAVID
- **Meaning:** "Beloved"
- **Context:** Youngest son of Jesse
- **Significance:** God's chosen king
* **New Testament parallel:** Christ as beloved Son (Matthew 3:17)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "And David was the seventh"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about birth order
**Biblical context:** This is about divine selection - David being seventh among Jesse's sons shows God's choice of the younger over the elder, demonstrating how divine selection often follows human patterns.

### "And anointed David king over them"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about political coronation
**Biblical context:** This is about divine appointment - David's anointing was God's choice, not human political maneuvering, showing how God establishes His chosen leaders.
