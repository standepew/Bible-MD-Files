### AMNON

- **Context:** Son who succeeded David
- **Significance:** Wise king who built temple

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "The firstborn Amnon, of Ahinoam the Jezreelitess"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about royal privilege
**Biblical context:** This is about human sinfulness - Amnon being firstborn didn't prevent his sin, showing how birthright doesn't guarantee righteousness.

### "And Solomon's son was Rehoboam"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about dynastic succession
**Biblical context:** This is about divine sovereignty - Solomon's lineage continued despite human failures, showing how God's purposes prevail.
