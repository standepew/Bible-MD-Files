3 And it came to pass the same night, that the word of God came to Nathan, saying,

5 And I have been with thee whithersoever thou hast walked, and have cut off all thine enemies from before thee, and have made thee a name like unto the name of the great men that are in the earth.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Lo, I dwell in an house of cedars, but the ark of the covenant of the LORD remaineth under curtains"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about material wealth
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual priorities - David's concern showed his desire for God's presence above personal comfort, demonstrating how spiritual priorities should guide earthly decisions.

### "Who am I, O LORD God? and what is mine house, that thou hast brought me hitherto?"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about self-doubt
**Biblical context:** This is about divine perspective - David's question showed his recognition of God's greatness and his own smallness, demonstrating how divine perspective brings humility.
