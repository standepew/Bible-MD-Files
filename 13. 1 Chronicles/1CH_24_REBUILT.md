## THE ACTUAL VERSES (KJV)

1 Now these are the divisions of the sons of Aaron. The sons of Aaron; Nadab, and Abihu, Eleazar, and Ithamar.  

2 But Nadab and Abihu died before their father, and had no children: therefore Eleazar and Ithamar executed the priest's office.  

3 And David distributed them, both Zadok of the sons of Eleazar, and Ahimelech of the sons of Ithamar, according to their offices in their service.  

4 And there were more chief men found of the sons of Eleazar than of the sons of Ithamar; and thus were they divided. Among the sons of Eleazar there were sixteen chief men of the house of their fathers, and eight among the sons of Ithamar according to the house of their fathers.  

5 Thus were they divided by lot, one sort with another; for the governors of the sanctuary, and governors of the house of God, were of the sons of Eleazar, and of the sons of Ithamar.  

6 And Shemaiah the son of Nethanel, the scribe, one of the Levites, wrote them before the king, and the princes, and Zadok the priest, and Ahimelech the son of Abiathar, and the chief of the fathers of the priests and Levites.  

7 The first lot came forth to Jehoiarib, the second to Jedaiah, the third to Harim, the fourth to Seorim, the fifth to Malchijah, the sixth to Mijamin, the seventh to Hakkoz, the eighth to Jeshua, the ninth to Jeshibab, the tenth to Jachin, the eleventh to Jachin, the twentieth to Gamul, the twentieth to Dalaiah, the twentieth to Maaziah.  

8 The orderings of them in their service to come into the house of the LORD were according to their manner, under Aaron their father, as the LORD God of Israel had commanded him.  

9 And the rest of the sons of Levi were these: Of the sons of Amram; Shubael, of the sons of Shubael; Jehdeiah.  

10 Concerning Rehabiah: of the sons of Rehabiah, the first was Isshiah.  

11 Of the Izharites; Shelomith, and Jahath.  

12 The sons of Hebron; Jeriah the first, Amariah the second, Jahaziel the third, and Jekameam the fourth.  

13 Of the sons of Uzziel; Michah the first, and Jesiah the second.  

14 The sons of Merari were Mahli and Mushi. The sons of Mahli; Eleazar, and Kish.  

15 And Eleazar died, and had no sons, but daughters: and their brethren the sons of Kish took them.  

16 The sons of Mushi; Mahli, and Eder, and Jerimoth.  

17 These were the sons of Levi after the house of their fathers; even the chief of the fathers, as they were counted by number of names by their polls, that did the work for the service of the house of the LORD, from the age of twenty years and upward.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "But Nadab and Abihu died before their father, and had no children: therefore Eleazar and Ithamar executed the priest's office"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about family tragedy
**Biblical context:** This is about divine judgment - Nadab and Abihu died for offering strange fire, showing how improper worship brings divine judgment.

### "Thus were they divided by lot, one sort with another"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about random selection
**Biblical context:** This is about divine selection - The lots determined God's choice, showing how divine sovereignty guides human decisions.
