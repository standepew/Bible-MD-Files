## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Moreover David and the captains of the host separated to the service of the sons of Asaph, and of Heman, and of Jeduthun, who should prophesy with harps, with psalteries, and with cymbals"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about musical entertainment
**Biblical context:** This is about divine worship - Temple musicians were set apart for holy service, showing how worship must be organized and dedicated to God.

### "So they cast lots, ward against ward, as well the small as the great, the teacher as the scholar"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about random selection
**Biblical context:** This is about divine order - The lots determined God's choice, showing how divine sovereignty guides human organization.
