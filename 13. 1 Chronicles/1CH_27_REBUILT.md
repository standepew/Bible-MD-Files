## THE ACTUAL VERSES (KJV)

1 Now children of Israel after their number, to wit, chief fathers and captains of thousands and hundreds, and their officers that served king in any matter of courses, which came in and went out month by month throughout all months of year, of every course were twenty and four thousand.  

2 Over first course for first month was Jashobeam son of Zabdiel: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.  

3 Of children of Perez was chief of all captains of host for first month.  

4 And over course of second month was Dodai an Ahohite, and of his course was Mikloth also a ruler: in his course likewise were twenty and four thousand.  

5 The third captain of host for third month was Benaiah son of Jehoiada, a chief priest: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.  

6 This is that Benaiah, who was mighty among thirty, and above thirty: and in his course was Ammizabad his son.  

7 The fourth captain for fourth month was Asahel brother of Joab, and Zebadiah his son after him: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.  

8 The fifth captain for fifth month was Shamhuth Izrahite: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.  

9 The sixth captain for sixth month was Ira son of Ikkesh Tekoite: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.  

10 The seventh captain for seventh month was Helez Pelonite, of the children of Ephraim: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.  

11 The eighth captain for eighth month was Sibbecai Hushathite, of Zarhites: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.  

12 The ninth captain for ninth month was Abi-ezer Anetothite, of Benjamites: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.  

13 The tenth captain for tenth month was Maharai Netophathite, of Zarhites: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.  

14 The eleventh captain for eleventh month was Benaiah Pirathonite, of the children of Ephraim: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.  

15 The twelfth captain for twelfth month was Heldai Netophathite, of Othniel: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.  

16 Furthermore over the tribes of Israel: ruler of Reubenites was Eliezer son of Zichri: of Simeonites, Shephatiah son of Maachah:  

17 Of Levites, Hashabiah son of Kemuel: of Aaronites, Zadok:  

18 Of Judah, Elihu, one of the brethren of David: of Issachar, Omri son of Michael:  

19 Of Zebulun, Ishmaiah son of Obadiah: of Naphtali, Jerimoth son of Azriel:  

20 Of the children of Ephraim, Hoshea son of Azaziah: of half tribe of Manasseh, Joel son of Pedaiah:  

21 Of half tribe of Manasseh in Gilead, Iddo son of Zechariah: of Benjamin, Jaasiel son of Abner:  

22 Of Dan, Azareel son of Jeroham. These were the princes of the tribes of Israel.  

23 But David took not number of them from twenty years old and under: because LORD had said he would increase Israel like to the stars of heavens.  

24 Joab son of Zeruiah began to number, but he finished not, because there fell wrath for it against Israel; neither was number put in account of chronicles of king David.  

25 And over the king's treasures was Azmaveth son of Adiel: and over storehouses in fields, in cities, and in villages, and in castles was Jehonathan son of Uzziah:  

26 And over them that did work of the field for tillage of the ground was Ezri son of Chelub:  

27 And over the vineyards was Shimei Ramathite: over the increase of the vineyards for wine cellars was Zabdi Shiphmite:  

28 And over the olive trees and sycamore trees that were in the low plains was Baal-hanan Gederite: and over the cellars of oil was Joash:  

29 And over the herds that fed in Sharon was Shitrai Sharonite: and over the herds that were in the valleys was Shaphat son of Adlai:  

30 Over the camels also was Obil Ishmaelite: and over the asses was Jehdeiah Meronothite:  

31 And over the flocks was Jaziz Hagerite. All these were the rulers of the substance which was king David's.  

32 Also Jonathan David's uncle was a counsellor, a wise man, and a scribe: and Jehiel son of Hachmoni was with the king's sons:  

33 And Ahithophel was the king's counsellor: and Hushai Archite was the king's companion:  

34 And after Ahithophel was Jehoiada son of Benaiah, and Abiathar: and the general of the king's army was Joab.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Now children of Israel after their number, to wit, chief fathers and captains of thousands and hundreds"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about military statistics
**Biblical context:** This is about divine organization - The military courses showed God's orderly plan for Israel's service, demonstrating how divine wisdom guides human organization.

### "But David took not number of them from twenty years old and under: because LORD had said he would increase Israel like to the stars of heavens"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about population growth
