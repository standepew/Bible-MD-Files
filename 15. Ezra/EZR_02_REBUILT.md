## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "These sought their register among those that were reckoned by genealogy, but they were not found: therefore were they, as polluted, put from the priesthood"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about ethnic purity
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual purity - Genealogical records were essential for priestly service, showing how spiritual identity requires divine verification.

### "And the Tirshatha said unto them, that they should not eat of the most holy things, till there stood up a priest with Urim and with Thummim"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about ceremonial exclusion
**Biblical context:** This is about divine authority - The Urim and Thummim represented God's guidance, showing how spiritual service requires divine approval.
