## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Now the city was large and great: but the people were few therein, and the houses were not builded"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about urban planning
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual readiness - The city was prepared but lacked population, showing how spiritual preparation requires people to fulfill God's purposes.

### "These sought their register among those that were reckoned by genealogy, but it was not found: therefore were they, as polluted, put from the priesthood"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about legalistic exclusion
**Biblical context:** This is about priestly purity - Those without proper lineage were excluded from priesthood, showing how spiritual service requires divine calling.
