- **Significance:** Model of divine presence

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "They clave to their brethren, their nobles, and entered into a curse, and into an oath, to walk in God's law"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about legalistic binding
**Biblical context:** This is about voluntary commitment - The people willingly bound themselves to God's law, showing how true commitment comes from the heart.

### "And if the people of the land bring ware or any victuals on the sabbath day to sell, that we would not buy it of them on the sabbath"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about economic regulation
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual priorities - The people refused commerce on Sabbath, showing how spiritual obedience requires practical application.
