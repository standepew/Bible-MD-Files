1 And the rulers of the people dwelt at Jerusalem: the rest of the people also cast lots, to bring one of ten to dwell in Jerusalem the holy city, and nine parts to dwell in other cities.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "And the people blessed all the men, that willingly offered themselves to dwell at Jerusalem"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about civic duty
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual sacrifice - These men willingly left their comfort to serve God's city, showing how true dedication requires personal sacrifice.

### "All the sons of Perez that dwelt at Jerusalem were four hundred threescore and eight valiant men"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about military strength
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual courage - These valiant men were strong in faith, showing how spiritual strength is more important than physical might.
