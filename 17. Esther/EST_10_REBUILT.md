## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "For Mordecai the Jew was next unto king Ahasuerus, and great among the Jews, and accepted of the multitude of his brethren"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about political ambition
**Biblical context:** This is about divine appointment - Mordecai's position came through God's providence, showing how God elevates faithful servants.

### "Seeking the wealth of his people, and speaking peace to all his seed"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about material prosperity
**Biblical context:** This is about servant leadership - Mordecai sought his people's welfare, showing how true leadership serves others.
