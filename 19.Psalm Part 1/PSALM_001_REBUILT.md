## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### BLESSED
- **Meaning:** "Happy, favored"
- **Context:** Spiritual prosperity from following God's ways
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual happiness
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual blessedness (Matthew 5:3)

### MEDITATE
- **Meaning:** "To ponder deeply"
- **Context:** Day and night contemplation of God's law
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual discipline
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual meditation (Joshua 1:8)

### TREE
- **Meaning:** "Plant with deep roots"
- **Context:** Symbol of spiritual stability and fruitfulness
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual growth
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual tree (John 15:5)

### CHAFF
- **Meaning:** "Worthless husk"
- **Context:** Symbol of spiritual emptiness and instability
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual worthlessness
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual chaff (Matthew 3:12)

## THE ACTUAL VERSES (KJV)

1 Blessed is the man that walketh not in the counsel of the ungodly, nor standeth in the way of sinners, nor sitteth in the seat of the scornful.  

2 But his delight is in the law of the LORD; and in his law doth he meditate day and night.  

3 And he shall be like a tree planted by the rivers of water, that bringeth forth his fruit in his season; his leaf also shall not wither; and whatsoever he doeth shall prosper.  

4 The ungodly are not so: but are like the chaff which the wind driveth away.  

5 Therefore the ungodly shall not stand in the judgment, nor sinners in the congregation of the righteous.  

6 For the LORD knoweth the way of the righteous: but the way of the ungodly shall perish.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Blessed is the man that walketh not in the counsel of the ungodly"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about social isolation
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual separation - The blessed person separates from ungodly counsel, showing spiritual discernment.

### "He shall be like a tree planted by the rivers of water"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about agricultural prosperity
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual vitality - The tree symbolizes spiritual stability and fruitfulness from God's provision.
