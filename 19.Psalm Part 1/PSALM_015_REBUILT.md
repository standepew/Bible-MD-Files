## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "He that walketh uprightly, and worketh righteousness, and speaketh the truth in his heart"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about earning salvation
**Biblical context:** This is about qualification for fellowship - These character marks show evidence of true worship, not means of earning salvation.

### "He that doeth these things shall never be moved"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about physical stability
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual security - Those with godly character have eternal stability, showing divine preservation.
