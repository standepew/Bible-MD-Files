## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### MICHTAM
- **Meaning:** "Golden psalm"
- **Context:** Musical term for precious composition
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual value
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual gold (1 Peter 1:7)

### PORTION
- **Meaning:** "Share, inheritance"
- **Context:** God as inheritance
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual provision
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual portion (Colossians 1:12)

### HOLY ONE
- **Meaning:** "Set apart one"
- **Context:** Reference to Messiah
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual holiness
* **New Testament parallel:** Christ as Holy One (Acts 3:14)

### CORRUPTION
- **Meaning:** "Decay, decomposition"
- **Context:** Physical death
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual death
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual corruption (2 Peter 2:12)

## THE ACTUAL VERSES (KJV)

1 Michtam of David. Preserve me, O God: for in thee do I put my trust.  

2 O my soul, thou hast said unto the LORD, Thou art my Lord: my goodness extendeth not to thee;  

3 But to the saints that are in the earth, and to the excellent, in whom is all my delight.  

4 Their sorrows shall be multiplied that hasten after another god: their drink offerings of blood will I not offer, nor take up their names into my lips.  

5 The LORD is the portion of mine inheritance and of my cup: thou maintainest my lot.  

6 The lines are fallen unto me in pleasant places; yea, I have a goodly heritage.  

7 I will bless the LORD, who hath given me counsel: my reins also instruct me in the night seasons.  

8 I have set the LORD always before me: because he is at my right hand, I shall not be moved.  

9 Therefore my heart is glad, and my glory rejoiceth: my flesh also shall rest in hope.  

10 For thou wilt not leave my soul in hell; neither wilt thou suffer thine Holy One to see corruption.  

11 Thou wilt shew me the path of life: in thy presence is fulness of joy; at thy right hand there are pleasures for evermore.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "The LORD is the portion of mine inheritance and of my cup"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about material blessing
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual satisfaction - God Himself is the inheritance, showing complete fulfillment in divine presence.

### "For thou wilt not leave my soul in hell; neither wilt thou suffer thine Holy One to see corruption"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about David's personal resurrection
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's resurrection - Peter and Paul apply this to Christ, showing Messianic prophecy.
