## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "The LORD is my shepherd; I shall not want"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about material prosperity
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual provision - God's shepherding care provides all spiritual needs, showing divine sufficiency.

### "Yea, though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death, I will fear no evil"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about avoiding death
**Biblical context:** This is about facing death with confidence - Walking through the valley shows courage in death, not avoidance.
