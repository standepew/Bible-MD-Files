## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### INTEGRITY
- **Meaning:** "Moral wholeness"
- **Context:** Upright character
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual purity
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual integrity (Acts 24:16)

### DISSEMBERS
- **Meaning:** "Deceivers, hypocrites"
- **Context:** Those who pretend to be what they're not
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual hypocrisy
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual hypocrisy (Matthew 23:27)

### INNOCENCY
- **Meaning:** "Freedom from guilt"
- **Context:** Moral purity
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual purity
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual innocence (Matthew 10:16)

### ALTAR
- **Meaning:** "Place of sacrifice"
- **Context:** Place of worship
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual worship
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual altar (Hebrews 13:10)

## THE ACTUAL VERSES (KJV)

1 Judge me, O LORD; for I have walked in mine integrity: I have trusted also in the LORD; therefore I shall not slide.  

2 Examine me, O LORD, and prove me; try my reins and my heart.  

3 For thy lovingkindness is before mine eyes: and I have walked in thy truth.  

4 I have not sat with vain persons, neither will I go in with dissemblers.  

5 I have hated the congregation of evil doers; and will not sit with the wicked.  

6 I will wash mine hands in innocency: so will I compass thine altar, O LORD:  

7 That I may publish with the voice of thanksgiving, and tell of all thy wondrous works.  

8 LORD, I have loved the habitation of thy house, and the place where thine honour dwelleth.  

9 Gather not my soul with sinners, nor my life with bloody men:  

10 In whose hands is mischief, and their right hand is full of bribes.  

11 But as for me, I will walk in mine integrity: redeem me, and be merciful unto me.  

12 My foot standeth in an even place: in the congregations will I bless the LORD.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "I will wash mine hands in innocency: so will I compass thine altar, O LORD"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about ritual purity
**Biblical context:** This is about moral integrity - Hand washing represents moral purity, not just ceremonial cleansing.

### "My foot standeth in an even place"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about physical stability
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual security - Even place represents divine stability, not just level ground.
