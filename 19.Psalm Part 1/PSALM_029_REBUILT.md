## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Give unto the LORD, O ye mighty, give unto the LORD glory and strength"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about human strength
**Biblical context:** This is about divine worship - The mighty give God glory, showing all power belongs to God.

### "The voice of the LORD is powerful; the voice of the LORD is full of majesty"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about physical thunder
**Biblical context:** This is about divine authority - God's voice represents His sovereign power, not just weather.
