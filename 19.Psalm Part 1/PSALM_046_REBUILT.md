## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### REFUGE
- **Meaning:** "Shelter, protection"
- **Context:** God's protection in trouble
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual security
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual refuge (Hebrews 6:18)

### ALAMOTH
- **Meaning:** "Maidens, young women"
- **Context:** Musical designation
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual praise
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual praise (Ephesians 5:19)

### TABERNACLES
- **Meaning:** "Dwelling places"
- **Context:** God's holy dwelling
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual presence
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual tabernacles (Hebrews 9:2)

### STILL
- **Meaning:** "Quiet, calm"
- **Context:** Spiritual tranquility
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual peace
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual stillness (Mark 4:39)

## THE ACTUAL VERSES (KJV)

1 To the chief Musician for the sons of Korah, A Song upon Alamoth. God is our refuge and strength, a very present help in trouble.  

2 Therefore will not we fear, though the earth be removed, and though the mountains be carried into the midst of the sea;  

3 Though the waters thereof roar and be troubled, though the mountains shake with the swelling thereof. Selah.  

4 There is a river, the streams whereof shall make glad the city of God, the holy place of the tabernacles of the most High.  

5 God is in the midst of her; she shall not be moved: God shall help her, and that right early.  

6 The heathen raged, the kingdoms were moved: he uttered his voice, the earth melted.  

7 The LORD of hosts is with us; the God of Jacob is our refuge. Selah.  

8 Come, behold the works of the LORD, what desolations he hath made in the earth.  

9 He maketh wars to cease unto the end of the earth; he breaketh the bow, and cutteth the spear in sunder; he burneth the chariot in the fire.  

10 Be still, and know that I am God: I will be exalted among the heathen, I will be exalted in the earth.  

11 The LORD of hosts is with us; the God of Jacob is our refuge. Selah.

* **Spiritual refuge:* Spiritual protection (Hebrews 6:18)

- **Physical no fear:** Earth removed (Psalm 46:2)

- **Physical presence:** God in midst (Psalm 46:5)
* **Spiritual presence:* Spiritual fellowship (Matthew 28:20)
* **Christ's presence:* Perfect fellowship (John 14:20)
* **Eternal presence:* Perfect communion (Revelation 22)

* **Christ's peace:* Perfect peace (John 14:27)
* **Eternal peace:* Perfect tranquility (Revelation 22)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "God is our refuge and strength, a very present help in trouble"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about emotional comfort
**Biblical context:** This is about divine protection - God is active help, not passive comfort, showing divine presence.

### "Be still, and know that I am God"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about meditation
**Biblical context:** This is about divine sovereignty - Stillness recognizes God's control, showing His supreme authority.
