## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### ZIPHIMS
- **Meaning:** "Inhabitants of Ziph"
- **Context:** Those who betrayed David
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual betrayal
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual betrayal (Judas)

### OPPRESSORS
- **Meaning:** "Those who burden others"
- **Context:** Enemies seeking David's life
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual persecution
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual oppressors (2 Timothy 3:12)

### HELPER
- **Meaning:** "One who assists"
- **Context:** God's assistance
- **Significance:** Model of divine help
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual helper (Hebrews 13:6)

### FREELY
- **Meaning:** "Voluntarily, willingly"
- **Context:** Willing sacrifice
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual generosity
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual freedom (2 Corinthians 9:7)

## THE ACTUAL VERSES (KJV)

1 To the chief Musician on Neginoth, Maschil, A Psalm of David, when the Ziphims came and said to Saul, Doth not David hide himself with us? Save me, O God, by thy name, and judge me by thy strength.  

2 Hear my prayer, O God; give ear to the words of my mouth.  

3 For strangers are risen up against me, and oppressors seek after my soul: they have not set God before them. Selah.  

4 Behold, God is mine helper: the Lord is with them that uphold my soul.  

5 He shall reward evil unto mine enemies: cut them off in thy truth.  

6 I will freely sacrifice unto thee: I will praise thy name, O LORD; for it is good.  

7 For he hath delivered me out of all trouble: and mine eye hath seen his desire upon mine enemies.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Behold, God is mine helper: the Lord is with them that uphold my soul"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about human assistance
**Biblical context:** This is about divine help - God as helper shows divine assistance, not human support.

### "I will freely sacrifice unto thee: I will praise thy name, O LORD; for it is good"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about ritual sacrifice
**Biblical context:** This is about willing worship - Freely sacrifice represents willing heart, not just ritual.
