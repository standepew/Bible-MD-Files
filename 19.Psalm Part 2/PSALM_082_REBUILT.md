## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "I have said, Ye are gods; and all of you are children of the most High"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about deity
**Biblical context:** This is about human authority - Ye are gods represents human judges, not divine beings.

### "But ye shall die like men, and fall like one of the princes"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about physical death only
**Biblical context:** This is about human mortality - Die like men represents human limitation, not just physical death.
