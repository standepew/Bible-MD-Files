## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "The LORD loveth the gates of Zion more than all the dwellings of Jacob"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about geographic preference
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual priority - Gates of Zion represent heavenly entrance, not just location.

### "I will make mention of Rahab and Babylon to them that know me"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about national pride
**Biblical context:** This is about divine inclusion - Nations mentioned represent universal salvation, not just nationalism.
