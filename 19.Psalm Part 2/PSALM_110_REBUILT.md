## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "The LORD said unto my Lord, Sit thou at my right hand, until I make thine enemies thy footstool"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about David
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's exaltation - My Lord refers to Messiah, not just David.

### "Thou art a priest for ever after the order of Melchizedek"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about earthly priesthood
**Biblical context:** This is about eternal priesthood - Order of Melchizedek represents divine priesthood, not just human.
