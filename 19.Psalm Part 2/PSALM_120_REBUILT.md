## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Woe is me, that I sojourn in Mesech, that I dwell in the tents of Kedar"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about geography
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual exile - Sojourn in Mesech represents living among hostile people, not just location.

### "I am for peace: but when I speak, they are for war"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about conflict
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual opposition - For peace speak war represents righteous witness, not just politics.
