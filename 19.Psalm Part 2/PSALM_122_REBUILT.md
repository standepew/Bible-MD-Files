## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Jerusalem is builded as a city that is compact together"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about urban planning
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual unity - Compact together represents spiritual community, not just architecture.

### "Pray for the peace of Jerusalem: they shall prosper that love thee"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about politics
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual prosperity - Peace of Jerusalem represents divine blessing, not just politics.
