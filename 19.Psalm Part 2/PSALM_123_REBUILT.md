## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Behold, as the eyes of servants look unto the hand of their masters"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about social hierarchy
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual dependence - Eyes of servants represents humble trust, not just status.

### "Our soul is exceedingly filled with the scorning of those that are at ease"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about social injustice
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual persecution - Scorn of ease represents spiritual pride, not just wealth.
