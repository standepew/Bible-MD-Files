- **Meaning:** "Proud, arrogant"
- **Context:** Spiritual pride
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual humility

### LOFTY
- **Meaning:** "Elevated, proud"
- **Context:** Spiritual arrogance
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual meekness
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual lofty (Psalm 25:9)

### WEANED
- **Meaning:** "Separated from nursing"
- **Context:** Spiritual maturity
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual growth
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual weaned (1 Peter 2:2)

### QUIETED
- **Meaning:** "Calmed, stilled"
- **Context:** Spiritual peace
- **Significance:** Model of spiritual tranquility
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual quieted (Matthew 11:29)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "LORD, my heart is not haughty, nor mine eyes lofty"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about self-esteem
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual humility - Heart not haughty represents meekness, not just confidence.

### "Surely I have behaved and quieted myself, as a child that is weaned of his mother"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about childhood
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual maturity - Weaned child represents spiritual contentment, not just age.
