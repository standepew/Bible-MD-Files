## THE ACTUAL VERSES (KJV)

1 A Song of degrees of David. Behold, how good and how pleasant it is for brethren to dwell together in unity!  

2 It is like the precious ointment upon the head, that ran down upon the beard, even Aaron's beard: that went down to the skirts of his garments.  

3 As the dew of Hermon, and as the dew that descended upon the mountains of Zion: for there the LORD commanded the blessing, even life for evermore.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Behold, how good and how pleasant it is for brethren to dwell together in unity!"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about social harmony
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual fellowship - Brethren dwell together represents spiritual unity, not just friendship.

### "It is like the precious ointment upon the head, that ran down upon the beard, even Aaron's beard"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about cosmetics
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual consecration - Precious ointment represents divine blessing, not just appearance.
