## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Behold, bless ye the LORD, all ye servants of the LORD, which by night stand in the house of the LORD"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about temple service
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual devotion - Night stand represents spiritual vigilance, not just duty.

### "The LORD that made heaven and earth bless thee out of Zion"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about creation
**Biblical context:** This is about divine blessing - Made heaven earth represents divine authority, not just nature.
