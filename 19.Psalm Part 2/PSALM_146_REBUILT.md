## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Put not your trust in princes, nor in the son of man, in whom there is no help"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about politics
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual dependence - Trust in princes represents human reliance, not just government.

### "His breath goeth forth, he returneth to his earth; in that very day his thoughts perish"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about death
**Biblical context:** This is about human mortality - Breath goeth represents divine life, not just biology.
