## CHAPTER SUMMARY

God tells Moses He has called Bezaleel by name — from the tribe of Judah — and filled him with the Spirit of God "in wisdom, and in understanding, and in knowledge, and in all manner of workmanship." Alongside him, Aholiab from the tribe of Dan. Together with all the wise-hearted, they will build the entire Tabernacle, its furniture, the priestly garments, the anointing oil, and the incense. Then God declares the Sabbath command again, now as the sign of the covenant: "it is a sign between me and you throughout your generations; that ye may know that I am the LORD that doth sanctify you." The Sabbath is "a perpetual covenant" — death for violation. God then gives Moses "two tables of testimony, tables of stone, written with the finger of God."

## CROSS-REFERENCES (Scripture Interpreting Scripture)

### Bezaleel — Called by Name, Filled with the Spirit for Craftsmanship

**Exodus 31:2-3** — "I have called him by name Bezaleel... And I have filled him with the spirit of God, in wisdom, and in understanding, and in knowledge, and in all manner of workmanship"

- The first person in the Bible explicitly described as being filled with the Spirit of God
- **Isaiah 11:2** — The Messianic Spirit: "the spirit of wisdom and understanding, the spirit of counsel and might, the spirit of knowledge and of the fear of the LORD" — the same triad: wisdom, understanding, knowledge
- **Ephesians 4:11-12** — God gives gifts for "the work of the ministry, for the edifying of the body of Christ" — the Spirit-gifted craftsman of Exodus is the type of the Spirit-gifted minister
- **1 Corinthians 12:4-11** — "the same Spirit... dividing to every man severally as he will" — diverse gifts from one Spirit; craftsmanship is a spiritual gift
- **Exodus 35:30-35** — Moses presents Bezaleel to the congregation: God has filled him with the Spirit, not merely with skill — the Spirit is the source of sacred artistry
- *Interpretation:* Bezaleel is the first person in Scripture identified as Spirit-filled — and his filling is for craftsmanship, not prophecy or warfare or leadership. This is a foundational declaration: the Spirit of God is given for the building of God's dwelling place, and artistry in service of the sanctuary is as Spirit-dependent as any other ministry. "All manner of workmanship" — no skill involved in building for God is outside the Spirit's domain. The three gifts (wisdom, understanding, knowledge) parallel the Messianic Spirit of Isaiah 11:2, pointing to Christ as the ultimate artisan who builds the living temple (1 Peter 2:5).

### Judah and Dan — The Two Craftsmen's Tribes

**Exodus 31:2, 6** — Bezaleel of Judah; Aholiab of Dan

- Judah = the royal tribe; the tribe of the king and ultimately of the Messiah
- Dan = the tribe of the judge; in Numbers 2, Judah leads the east and Dan leads the north
- The two master craftsmen come from opposite ends of the tribal arrangement: Judah (first) and Dan (last in some orderings)
- *Interpretation:* God chooses the lead craftsmen from opposite ends of Israel's tribal structure — the royal tribe and the furthest from it — signaling that the building of God's house draws from every part of Israel. Neither rank nor tribe earns the Spirit's gifting; it is given sovereignly to those God calls by name.

### "I Have Called Him by Name" — Divine Calling Before Birth

**Exodus 31:2** — "See, I have called by name Bezaleel"

- **Isaiah 43:1** — "Fear not: for I have redeemed thee, I have called thee by thy name; thou art mine"
- **Isaiah 49:1** — "The LORD hath called me from the womb; from the bowels of my mother hath he made mention of my name"
- **Jeremiah 1:5** — "Before I formed thee in the belly I knew thee; and before thou camest forth out of the womb I sanctified thee"
- **John 10:3** — "the sheep hear his voice: and he calleth his own sheep by name"
- *Interpretation:* Divine calling is personal and specific. God does not say "find a craftsman" — He says "I have called Bezaleel by name." The name already existed in God's intention before the commission was given to Moses. This is the pattern of all divine calling: the prophet, the servant, the apostle, the craftsman — all called by name before their calling is announced to men.

### The Sabbath as the Sign of Covenant Sanctification

**Exodus 31:13** — "Verily my sabbaths ye shall keep: for it is a sign between me and you throughout your generations; that ye may know that I am the LORD that doth sanctify you"

- The Sabbath is not merely rest — it is the visible sign that YHWH is the one who sanctifies Israel; not Israel's own effort
- **Ezekiel 20:12** — "Moreover also I gave them my sabbaths, to be a sign between me and them, that they might know that I am the LORD that sanctify them"
- **Ezekiel 20:20** — "And hallow my sabbaths; and they shall be a sign between me and you, that ye may know that I am the LORD your God"
- **Hebrews 4:9-10** — "There remaineth therefore a rest (sabbatismos) to the people of God. For he that is entered into his rest, he also hath ceased from his own works, as God did from his" — the Sabbath rest = ceasing from self-produced sanctification; resting in God's work
- *Interpretation:* The Sabbath's theological core is stated here: it is the sign that God sanctifies — not Israel's own industry. To keep the Sabbath is to declare "I trust God to provide and to sanctify; I am not the source of my own standing." This is the anti-works-righteousness sign embedded in the Law itself. Hebrews 4 applies it to salvation: entering God's rest means ceasing from works-righteousness and resting in Christ's completed work. Every Sabbath was a weekly enacted declaration: "God sanctifies; I do not sanctify myself."

---

### "Written with the Finger of God"

**Exodus 31:18** — "two tables of testimony, tables of stone, written with the finger of God"

- **Exodus 8:19** — Pharaoh's magicians: "This is the finger of God" — the plagues were divine power
- **Luke 11:20** — Jesus: "But if I with the finger of God cast out devils, no doubt the kingdom of God is come upon you" — the same phrase; Jesus identifies His works as the finger of God
- **2 Corinthians 3:3** — "written not with ink, but with the Spirit of the living God; not in tables of stone, but in fleshy tables of the heart" — the New Covenant is the law written by the same divine "finger" (the Spirit) but now on hearts, not stone
- *Interpretation:* "The finger of God" is direct divine agency — no intermediary, no prophet, no scribe. The Law carved on stone by God's finger is the type of the Law written by the Spirit on the heart (Jeremiah 31:33; 2 Corinthians 3:3). The same God who inscribed stone with His finger now writes on living human hearts by His Spirit. The medium changes; the Author is the same.

## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### BEZALEEL (Hebrew: Betsal'el)
- Meaning: "In the shadow/protection of God" — a name that describes the one sheltered under God's wing
- Son of Uri (= my light/flame), son of Hur (= noble/free); grandson of Hur who held up Moses' arms at Rephidim (Exodus 17:12)
- *Definition here:* The Spirit-called craftsman of God's dwelling; a name signifying protection in God's shadow — fitting for one who works in the innermost sanctuary

### WISE-HEARTED (Hebrew: chakham-lev)
- **Exodus 31:6** — "all that are wise hearted I have put wisdom" — chakham = wise, skilled; lev = heart
- In Hebrew thought, the heart is the seat of intelligence and will, not merely emotion
- *Definition here:* Those in whom the capacity for skilled discernment and practical wisdom resides — the Spirit's gift of wisdom expressed through the hands and the mind working in concert

## THE ACTUAL VERSES — EXODUS 31 (KJV)

**1** And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying,
**2** See, I have called by name Bezaleel the son of Uri, the son of Hur, of the tribe of Judah:
**3** And I have filled him with the spirit of God, in wisdom, and in understanding, and in knowledge, and in all manner of workmanship,
**4** To devise cunning works, to work in gold, and in silver, and in brass,
**5** And in cutting of stones, to set them, and in carving of timber, to work in all manner of workmanship.
**6** And I, behold, I have given with him Aholiab, the son of Ahisamach, of the tribe of Dan: and in the hearts of all that are wise hearted I have put wisdom, that they may make all that I have commanded thee;
**7** The tabernacle of the congregation, and the ark of the testimony, and the mercy seat that is thereupon, and all the furniture of the tabernacle,
**8** And the table and his furniture, and the pure candlestick with all his furniture, and the altar of incense,
**9** And the altar of burnt offering with all his furniture, and the laver and his foot,
**10** And the cloths of service, and the holy garments for Aaron the priest, and the garments of his sons, to minister in the priest's office,
**11** And the anointing oil, and sweet incense for the holy place: according to all that I have commanded thee shall they do.
**12** And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying,
**13** Speak thou also unto the children of Israel, saying, Verily my sabbaths ye shall keep: for it is a sign between me and you throughout your generations; that ye may know that I am the LORD that doth sanctify you.
**14** Ye shall keep the sabbath therefore; for it is holy unto you: every one that defileth it shall surely be put to death: for whosoever doeth any work therein, that soul shall be cut off from among his people.
**15** Six days may work be done; but in the seventh is the sabbath of rest, holy to the LORD: whosoever doeth any work in the sabbath day, he shall surely be put to death.
**16** Wherefore the children of Israel shall keep the sabbath, to observe the sabbath throughout their generations, for a perpetual covenant.
**17** It is a sign between me and the children of Israel for ever: for in six days the LORD made heaven and earth, and on the seventh day he rested, and was refreshed.
**18** And he gave unto Moses, when he had made an end of communing with him upon mount Sinai, two tables of testimony, tables of stone, written with the finger of God.

## PROPHETIC/TYPOLOGICAL CONNECTIONS

### The Spirit-Filled Craftsman — Type of the Spirit-Gifted Body of Christ

- Bezaleel + Aholiab + the wise-hearted = a Spirit-equipped community building God's dwelling
- **1 Corinthians 3:9-17** — "Ye are God's building... ye are the temple of God, and the Spirit of God dwelleth in you"
- **Ephesians 2:20-22** — "built upon the foundation of the apostles and prophets, Jesus Christ himself being the chief corner stone... an holy temple in the Lord"
- *Type:* The building of the Tabernacle by Spirit-filled craftsmen is the type of the building of the New Covenant temple — the church — by Spirit-gifted believers. Every gift of the Spirit is a "Bezaleel gift" — given for the building of God's dwelling among His people.
