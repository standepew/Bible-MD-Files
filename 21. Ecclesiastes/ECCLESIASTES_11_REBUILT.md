## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### GENEROSITY
- **Meaning:** "Giving, liberality"
- **Context:** Investing in others with divine return
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's sacrificial giving
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual generosity (Luke 6:38)

### SOVEREIGNTY
- **Meaning:** "Divine control"
- **Context:** God's control over nature and life
- **Significance:** Model of God's perfect providence
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual sovereignty (James 5:7)

### LIMITATION
- **Meaning:** "Human inability"
- **Context:** Not knowing future or divine works
- **Significance:** Model of human need for faith
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual limitation (John 3:8)

### LIGHT
- **Meaning:** "Illumination, life"
- **Context:** Physical light as spiritual truth
- **Significance:** Model of Christ as light of world
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual light (John 8:12)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Cast thy bread upon the waters: for thou shalt find it after many days"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about fishing
**Biblical context:** This is about generosity - Cast bread represents investing in others, not just literal bread on water.

### "He that observeth the wind shall not sow"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about farming
**Biblical context:** This is about faith vs. fear - Observeth wind represents over-caution, not just agriculture.
