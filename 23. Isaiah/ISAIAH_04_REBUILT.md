## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### BRANCH
- **Meaning:** "Christ the Messiah, divine life"
- **Context:** The Branch of the LORD as beautiful and glorious
- **Significance:** Model of Christ as the living Branch
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual branch (John 15:5)

### HOLY
- **Meaning:** "Spiritual purity, set apart for God"
- **Context:** Those written among the living called holy
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's sanctifying work
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual holy (Revelation 20:12)

### CLEANSING
- **Meaning:** "Spiritual purification through Christ"
- **Context:** Washing away filth and purging blood
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's cleansing work
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual cleansing (Titus 3:5)

### TABERNACLE
- **Meaning:** "Divine presence, spiritual protection"
- **Context:** God's tabernacle providing shadow and refuge
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's protective presence
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual tabernacle (Hebrews 9:11)

## THE ACTUAL VERSES (KJV)

1 And in that day seven women shall take hold of one man, saying, We will eat our own bread, and wear our own apparel: only let us be called by thy name, to take away our reproach.  

2 In that day shall the branch of the LORD be beautiful and glorious, and the fruit of the earth shall be excellent and comely for them that are escaped of Israel.  

3 And it shall come to pass, that he that is left in Zion, and he that remaineth in Jerusalem, shall be called holy, even every one that is written among the living in Jerusalem.  

4 When the Lord shall have washed away the filth of the daughters of Zion, and shall have purged the blood of Jerusalem from the midst thereof by the spirit of judgment, and by the spirit of burning.  

5 And the LORD will create upon every dwelling place of mount Zion, and upon her assemblies, a cloud and smoke by day, and the shining of a flaming fire by night: for upon all the glory shall be a defence.  

6 And there shall be a tabernacle for a shadow in the daytime from the heat, and for a place of refuge, and for a covert from storm and from rain.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "In that day shall the branch of the LORD be beautiful and glorious"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about plant growth
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ the Messiah - Branch LORD represents Christ as the living Branch, not just botany.

### "When the Lord shall have washed away the filth of the daughters of Zion"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about physical cleanliness
**Biblical context:** This is about spiritual purification - Washed filth represents Christ's cleansing work, not just hygiene.
