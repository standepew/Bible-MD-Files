## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### SALVATION
- **Meaning:** "Divine deliverance, Christ's redemptive work"
- **Context:** God as salvation and source of living water
- **Significance:** Model of Christ as Savior and source of spiritual life
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual salvation (John 4:14)

### PRAISE
- **Meaning:** "Divine worship, spiritual adoration"
- **Context:** Response to God's salvation with praise and testimony
- **Significance:** Model of worship for Christ's redemptive work
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual praise (Romans 10:13)

### JOY
- **Meaning:** "Spiritual delight, divine gladness"
- **Context:** Joy in drawing from wells of salvation
- **Significance:** Model of joy in Christ's salvation
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual joy (Philippians 4:4)

### STRENGTH
- **Meaning:** "Divine power, spiritual might"
- **Context:** LORD as strength and song for believers
- **Significance:** Model of Christ as source of spiritual strength
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual strength (Philippians 4:13)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Therefore with joy shall ye draw water out of the wells of salvation"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about physical water
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's living water - Wells salvation represents Christ's spiritual life, not just hydration.

### "The LORD JEHOVAH is my strength and my song"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about human ability
**Biblical context:** This is about divine empowerment - Strength song represents God's power, not just human capability.
