## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### WATCHFULNESS
- **Meaning:** "Christ's vigilance, spiritual alertness"
- **Context:** Watchmen on Jerusalem's walls who never hold their peace
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's constant intercession
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual watchfulness (Ezekiel 33:7)

### PROTECTION
- **Meaning:** "Divine defense, Christ's care"
- **Context:** God's sworn protection of Jerusalem's provision
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's protective covenant
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual protection (Hebrews 6:13)

### RESTORATION
- **Meaning:** "Divine renewal, spiritual transformation"
- **Context:** Transformation from Forsaken to Hephzi-bah and Desolate to Beulah
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's restoring work
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual restoration (Hosea 2:16)

### MARRIAGE
- **Meaning:** "Divine union, Christ's relationship"
- **Context:** Marriage between God and His people as bridegroom and bride
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's intimate relationship
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual marriage (Romans 7:4)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "For Zion's sake will I not hold my peace"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any peace
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's intercessory watchfulness - Hold peace represents spiritual vigilance, not just rest.

### "Thou shalt be called Hephzi-bah, and thy land Beulah"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any names
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's spiritual transformation - Names represent divine relationship, not just labels.
