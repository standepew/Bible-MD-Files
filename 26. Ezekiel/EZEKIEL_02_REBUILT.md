## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### CALLING
- **Meaning:** "Divine appointment, Christ's selection"
- **Context:** God's calling of Ezekiel as a prophet to speak His word
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's calling of His servants to ministry
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual calling (Daniel 7:13)

### MISSION
- **Meaning:** "Divine assignment, Christ's purpose"
- **Context:** The mission to speak to rebellious Israel despite their rejection
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's mission to reach the rebellious
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual mission (Jeremiah 7:25)

### COMMISSION
- **Meaning:** "Divine empowerment, Christ's authority"
- **Context:** God's commission to speak His word without fear
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's commission to His followers
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual commission (2 Corinthians 11:26)

### AUTHORITY
- **Meaning:** "Divine power, Christ's rule"
- **Context:** God's authority in sending the prophet and giving the message
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's divine authority over His word
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual authority (Revelation 5:1)
