## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### JUDGMENT
- **Meaning:** "Divine punishment, Christ's wrath"
- **Context:** The judgment of Jerusalem as a useless vine fit only for fire
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's judgment on unfruitful people
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual judgment (John 15:1)

### VINE
- **Meaning:** "Spiritual fruitfulness, Christ's people"
- **Context:** The vine tree that is useless for any work except fuel
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's people who bear no fruit
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual vine (Psalm 80:8)

### FIRE
- **Meaning:** "Divine purification, Christ's judgment"
- **Context:** The fire that consumes the useless vine wood
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's purifying judgment
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual fire (Hebrews 12:29)

### WRATH
- **Meaning:** "Divine anger, Christ's displeasure"
- **Context:** God's wrath against Jerusalem for their trespass
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's righteous anger against sin
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual wrath (Romans 1:18)

## THE ACTUAL VERSES (KJV)

1 And the word of the LORD came unto me, saying,  

2 Son of man, What is the vine tree more than any tree, or than a branch which is among the trees of the forest?  

3 Shall wood be taken thereof to do any work? or will men take a pin of it to hang any vessel thereon?  

4 Behold, it is cast into the fire for fuel; the fire devoureth both the ends of it, and the midst of it is burned. Is it meet for any work?  

5 Behold, when it was whole, it was meet for no work: how much less shall it be meet yet for any work, when the fire hath devoured it, and it is burned?  

6 Therefore thus saith the Lord GOD; As the vine tree among the trees of the forest, which I have given to the fire for fuel, so will I give the inhabitants of Jerusalem.  

7 And I will set my face against them; they shall go out from one fire, and another fire shall devour them; and ye shall know that I am the LORD, when I set my face against them.  

8 And I will make the land desolate, because they have committed a trespass, saith the Lord GOD.  

9 And I will make the land desolate, because they have committed a trespass, saith the Lord GOD.  

10 And I will make the land desolate, because they have committed a trespass, saith the Lord GOD.  

11 And I will make the land desolate, because they have committed a trespass, saith the Lord GOD.

**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any agricultural comparison
