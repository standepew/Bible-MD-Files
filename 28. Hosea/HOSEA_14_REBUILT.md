## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### RESTORATION
- **Meaning:** "Divine renewal, Christ's salvation"
- **Context:** God's call for Israel to return and His promise of healing and restoration
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's restoration and healing of backsliding people
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual restoration (Joel 2:12)

### HEALING
- **Meaning:** "Divine cure, Christ's restoration"
- **Context:** God's healing of backsliding and His presence as refreshing dew
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's healing and restoration through His presence
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual healing (Jeremiah 3:22)

### FRUITFULNESS
- **Meaning:** "Divine abundance, Christ's blessing"
- **Context:** The fruitfulness that comes from abiding in God and rejecting idols
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's fruitfulness and blessing for His people
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual fruitfulness (Romans 11:17)

### WISDOM
- **Meaning:** "Divine understanding, Christ's knowledge"
- **Context:** The wisdom needed to understand God's ways and walk in them
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's wisdom and the righteous path
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual wisdom (Proverbs 9:10)

## THE ACTUAL VERSES (KJV)

1 O Israel, return unto the LORD thy God; for thou hast fallen by thine iniquity.  

2 Take with you words, and turn to the LORD: say unto him, Take away all iniquity, and receive us graciously: so will we render the calves of our lips.  

3 Asshur shall not save us; we will not ride upon horses: neither will we say any more to the work of our hands, Ye are our gods: for in thee the fatherless findeth mercy.  

4 I will heal their backsliding, I will love them freely: for mine anger is turned away from him.  

5 I will be as the dew unto Israel: he shall grow as the lily, and cast forth his roots as Lebanon.  

6 His branches shall spread, and his beauty shall be as the olive tree, and his smell as Lebanon.  

7 They that dwell under his shadow shall return; they shall revive as the corn, and grow as the vine: the scent thereof shall be as the wine of Lebanon.  

8 Ephraim shall say, What have I to do any more with idols? I have heard him, and observed him: I am like a green fir tree. From me is thy fruit found.  

9 Who is wise, and he shall understand these things? prudent, and he shall know them? for the ways of the LORD are right, and the just shall walk in them: but the transgressors shall fall therein.  

10 Who is wise, and he shall understand these things? prudent, and he shall know them? for the ways of the LORD are right, and the just shall walk in them: but the transgressors shall fall therein.  

11 Who is wise, and he shall understand these things? prudent, and he shall know them? for the ways of the LORD are right, and the just shall walk in them: but the transgressors shall fall therein.  

12 Who is wise, and he shall understand these things? prudent, and he shall know them? for the ways of the LORD are right, and the just shall walk in them: but the transgressors shall fall therein.

**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any verbal apology or confession
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's restoration - The calves of lips represent true worship and praise instead of animal sacrifices.

### "I will be as the dew unto Israel: he shall grow as the lily, and cast forth his roots as Lebanon"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any agricultural growth or natural beauty
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's restoration - The dew and growth represent spiritual restoration and fruitfulness.
