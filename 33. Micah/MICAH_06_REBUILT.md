## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### CONTROVERSY
- **Meaning:** "Divine dispute, Christ's confrontation"
- **Context:** God's controversy with His people for their ingratitude and injustice
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's confrontation with hypocrisy and false worship
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual controversy (Matthew 23:38)

### FAITHFULNESS
- **Meaning:** "Divine loyalty, Christ's covenant"
- **Context:** God's faithfulness in delivering Israel from Egypt and providing leaders
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's faithfulness and covenant love for His people
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual faithfulness (1 Corinthians 10:4)

### WORSHIP
- **Meaning:** "Divine service, Christ's devotion"
- **Context:** The contrast between external sacrifices and true heart worship
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's true worship and opposition to empty ritual
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual worship (Hebrews 10:4)

### JUSTICE
- **Meaning:** "Divine righteousness, Christ's fairness"
- **Context:** God's requirement for justice, mercy, and humility rather than ritual
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's justice and His call to righteous living
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual justice (Romans 12:1)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "He hath shewed thee, O man, what is good; and what doth the LORD require of thee, but to do justly, and to love mercy, and to walk humbly with thy God?"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any moral good works or social justice
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's righteousness - The requirements represent Christ's call to true worship through justice, mercy, and humility rather than empty ritual.

### "Will the LORD be pleased with thousands of rams, or with ten thousands of rivers of oil? shall I give my firstborn for my transgression"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any sacrificial system or religious offerings
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's devotion - The sacrifices represent the inadequacy of external rituals without true heart devotion.
