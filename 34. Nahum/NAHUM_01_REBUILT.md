## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### VENGEANCE
- **Meaning:** "Divine justice, Christ's wrath"
- **Context:** God's vengeance against His adversaries and Nineveh's wickedness
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's divine justice and righteous wrath against sin
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual vengeance (Romans 12:19)

### POWER
- **Meaning:** "Divine authority, Christ's strength"
- **Context:** God's great power in whirlwind, storm, and control over nature
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's divine authority and power over all creation
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual power (Matthew 8:26)

### PROTECTION
- **Meaning:** "Divine refuge, Christ's safety"
- **Context:** God as a stronghold for those who trust in Him during trouble
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's protection and refuge for the righteous
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual protection (Hebrews 6:18)

### DELIVERANCE
- **Meaning:** "Divine rescue, Christ's salvation"
- **Context:** The deliverance of Judah from the wicked and the good tidings of peace
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's deliverance and the gospel of peace
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual deliverance (Romans 10:15)

14 And the LORD hath given a commandment concerning thee, that no more of thy name be sown: out of the house of thy gods will I cut off the graven image and the molten image: I will make thy grave; for thou art vile.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "The LORD is slow to anger, and great in power, and will not at all acquit the wicked"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any patience or tolerance
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's authority - The slowness represents God's patience combined with His ultimate justice and power against wickedness.

### "The LORD is good, a strong hold in the day of trouble; and he knoweth them that trust in him"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any protection or safety
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's refuge - The stronghold represents God's protection for those who trust Him, contrasting with His judgment on the wicked.
