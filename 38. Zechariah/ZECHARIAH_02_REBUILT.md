## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### PROTECTION
- **Meaning:** "Divine refuge, Christ's safety"
- **Context:** God's protection of Jerusalem as a wall of fire and His care for His people
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's protection and His refuge for His church
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual protection (Deuteronomy 32:10)

### EXPANSION
- **Meaning:** "Divine growth, Christ's multiplication"
- **Context:** The expansion of Jerusalem beyond walls and the gathering of many people
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's growth of His church and multiplication of His people
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual expansion (Isaiah 60:4)

### PRESENCE
- **Meaning:** "Divine indwelling, Christ's communion"
- **Context:** God's promise to dwell in the midst of His people and His glory among them
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's indwelling presence and His communion with believers
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual presence (John 14:23)

### GATHERING
- **Meaning:** "Divine collection, Christ's assembly"
- **Context:** The gathering of nations to the LORD and their becoming His people
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's gathering of all nations to Himself
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual gathering (Revelation 7:9)

## THE ACTUAL VERSES (KJV)

1 I lifted up mine eyes again, and looked, and behold a man with a measuring line in his hand.  

2 Then said I, Whither goest thou? And he said unto me, To measure Jerusalem, to see what is the breadth thereof, and what is the length thereof.  

3 And, behold, the angel that talked with me went forth, and another angel went out to meet him,  

4 And said unto him, Run, speak to this young man, saying, Jerusalem shall be inhabited as towns without walls for the multitude of men and cattle therein:  

5 For I, saith the LORD, will be unto her a wall of fire round about, and will be the glory in the midst of her.  

6 Ho, ho, come forth, and flee from the land of the north, saith the LORD: for I have spread you abroad as the four winds of the heaven, saith the LORD.  

7 Deliver thyself, O Zion, that dwellest with the daughter of Babylon.  

8 For thus saith the LORD of hosts; After the glory hath he sent me unto the nations which spoiled you: for he that toucheth you toucheth the apple of his eye.  

9 For, behold, I will shake mine hand upon them, and they shall be a spoil to their servants: and ye shall know that the LORD of hosts hath sent me.  

10 Sing and rejoice, O daughter of Zion: for, lo, I come, and I will dwell in the midst of thee, saith the LORD.  

11 And many nations shall be joined to the LORD in that day, and shall be my people: and I will dwell in the midst of thee, and thou shalt know that the LORD of hosts hath sent me unto thee.  

12 And the LORD shall inherit Judah his portion in the holy land, and shall choose Jerusalem again.  

13 Be silent, O all flesh, before the LORD: for he is raised up out of his holy habitation.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "For I, saith the LORD, will be unto her a wall of fire round about, and will be the glory in the midst of her"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any protection or defense system
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's refuge - The wall of fire represents God's divine protection and Christ's presence as the ultimate safeguard for His people.

### "For he that toucheth you toucheth the apple of his eye"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any precious or valuable thing
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's care - The apple of the eye represents God's tender care for His people and Christ's special love for His church.
