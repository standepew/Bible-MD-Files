## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### CLEANSING
- **Meaning:** "Divine purification, Christ's salvation"
- **Context:** The cleansing of Joshua from filthy garments and clothing with righteousness
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's cleansing from sin and His salvation
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual cleansing (Revelation 7:14)

### FORGIVENESS
- **Meaning:** "Divine pardon, Christ's redemption"
- **Context:** God's forgiveness of iniquity and removal of sin in one day
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's forgiveness and complete redemption
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual forgiveness (1 Corinthians 3:15)

### BRANCH
- **Meaning:** "Divine Messiah, Christ's kingship"
- **Context:** The promise of the Branch who will be God's servant and ruler
- **Significance:** Model of Christ as the Messiah and His righteous rule
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual Branch (Jeremiah 23:5)

### STONE
- **Meaning:** "Divine foundation, Christ's church"
- **Context:** The stone with seven eyes representing complete oversight and foundation
- **Significance:** Model of Christ as the foundation stone and His complete knowledge
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual stone (1 Peter 2:4)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Behold, I have caused thine iniquity to pass from thee, and I will clothe thee with change of raiment"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any change of clothing or improvement
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's salvation - The change of raiment represents the removal of sin and the clothing with Christ's righteousness.

### "I will bring forth my servant the BRANCH"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any plant or branch
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's messiahship - The Branch represents the Messiah who will be God's servant and righteous ruler.
