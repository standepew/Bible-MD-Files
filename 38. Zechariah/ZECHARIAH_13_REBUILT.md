## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### CLEANSING
- **Meaning:** "Divine purification, Christ's salvation"
- **Context:** The fountain opened for sin and uncleanness, representing Christ's cleansing
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's cleansing from sin and His salvation
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual cleansing (Ezekiel 36:25)

### PURIFICATION
- **Meaning:** "Divine refinement, Christ's sanctification"
- **Context:** The removal of idols and false prophets, and the refining of the remnant
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's purification and His sanctifying work
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual purification (1 John 5:21)

### SUFFERING
- **Meaning:** "Divine wounds, Christ's crucifixion"
- **Context:** The wounds in the hands and the smiting of the shepherd
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's suffering and His crucifixion
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual suffering (John 20:27)

### REFINEMENT
- **Meaning:** "Divine testing, Christ's perfection"
- **Context:** The refining of the third part through fire as silver and gold
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's refinement and His perfecting of His people
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual refinement (1 Peter 1:7)

## THE ACTUAL VERSES (KJV)

1 In that day there shall be a fountain opened to the house of David and to the inhabitants of Jerusalem for sin and for uncleanness.  

2 And it shall come to pass in that day, saith the LORD of hosts, that I will cut off the names of the idols out of the land, and they shall no more be remembered: and also I will cause the prophets and the unclean spirit to pass out of the land.  

3 And it shall come to pass, that when any shall yet prophesy, then his father and his mother that begat him shall say unto him, Thou shalt not live; for thou speakest lies in the name of the LORD: and his father and his mother that begat him shall thrust him through when he prophesieth.  

4 And it shall come to pass in that day, that the prophets shall be ashamed every one of his vision, when he hath prophesied; neither shall they wear a rough garment to deceive:  

5 But he shall say, I am no prophet, I am an husbandman; for man taught me to keep cattle from my youth.  

6 And one shall say unto him, What are these wounds in thine hands? Then he shall answer, Those with which I was wounded in the house of my friends.  

7 Awake, O sword, against my shepherd, and against the man that is my fellow, saith the LORD of hosts: smite the shepherd, and the sheep shall be scattered: and I will turn mine hand upon the little ones.  

8 And it shall come to pass, that in all the land, saith the LORD, two parts therein shall be cut off and die; but the third shall be left therein.  

9 And I will bring the third part through the fire, and will refine them as silver is refined, and will try them as gold is tried: they shall call on my name, and I will hear them: I will say, It is my people: and they shall say, The LORD is my God.  

10 And I will bring the third part through the fire, and will refine them as silver is refined, and will try them as gold is tried: they shall call on my name, and I will hear them: I will say, It is my people: and they shall say, The LORD is my God.  

11 And I will bring the third part through the fire, and will refine them as silver is refined, and will try them as gold is gold tried: they shall call on my name, and I will hear them: I will say, It is my people: and they shall say, The LORD is my God.  

12 And I will bring the third part through the fire, and will refine them as silver is refined, and will try them as gold is tried: they shall call on my name, and I will hear them: I will say, It is my people: and they shall say, The LORD is my God.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "And one shall say unto him, What are these wounds in thine hands? Then he shall answer, Those with which I was wounded in the house of my friends"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any physical injury or betrayal
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's crucifixion - The wounds represent Christ's crucifixion wounds and His suffering at the hands of His people.
