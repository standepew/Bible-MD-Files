## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### GENEALOGY
- **Meaning:** "Divine lineage, Christ's kingship"
- **Context:** The royal genealogy from Abraham through David to Christ
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's royal lineage and His rightful kingship
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual genealogy (Romans 1:3)

### VIRGINITY
- **Meaning:** "Divine purity, Christ's incarnation"
- **Context:** The virgin birth of Jesus conceived by the Holy Ghost
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's divine incarnation and His sinless nature
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual virginity (Luke 1:35)

### SALVATION
- **Meaning:** "Divine deliverance, Christ's redemption"
- **Context:** The meaning of Jesus' name - He shall save His people from their sins
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's salvation and His redemptive work
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual salvation (Acts 4:12)

### PROTECTION
- **Meaning:** "Divine care, Christ's guidance"
- **Context:** The angelic protection and guidance given to Joseph
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's protection and His guidance of His people
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual protection (Matthew 2:13)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Behold, a virgin shall be with child, and shall bring forth a son, and they shall call his name Emmanuel"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any birth or child
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's incarnation - The virgin birth represents Christ's divine conception and His unique nature as both God and man.

### "And she shall bring forth a son, and thou shalt call his name JESUS: for he shall save his people from their sins"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any name or salvation
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's salvation - The name Jesus means "Jehovah saves" and represents His mission to save people from their sins.
