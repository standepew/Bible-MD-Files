## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### HEALING
- **Meaning:** "Divine restoration, Christ's power"
- **Context:** Christ's healing of the withered hand demonstrating His power over physical infirmities
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's power and His authority to restore what is broken
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual healing (Matthew 12:10)

### APOSTLESHIP
- **Meaning:** "Divine commission, Christ's mission"
- **Context:** The ordination of twelve disciples for preaching and healing ministry
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's mission and His establishment of the apostolic foundation
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual apostleship (Matthew 10:1)

### KINGDOM
- **Meaning:** "Divine reign, Christ's authority"
- **Context:** Christ's teaching on the unity of His kingdom against Satan's division
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's authority and His victory over spiritual opposition
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual kingdom (Matthew 12:25)

### FAMILY
- **Meaning:** "Divine relationship, Christ's fellowship"
- **Context:** Christ's definition of true family as those who do God's will
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's fellowship and His establishment of spiritual family bonds
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual family (Matthew 12:50)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "And he ordained twelve, that they should be with him, and that he might send them forth to preach"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any leadership or ministry
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's commission - The ordination represents Christ's establishment of the apostolic foundation for His church and mission.

### "For whosoever shall do the will of God, the same is my brother, and my sister, and mother"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any family relationship
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's fellowship - The family represents spiritual relationship based on obedience to God's will, not natural ties.
