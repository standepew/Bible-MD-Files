## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### DELIVERANCE
- **Meaning:** "Divine liberation, Christ's power"
- **Context:** Christ's deliverance of the demon-possessed man from multiple demons
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's power and His authority over spiritual forces
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual deliverance (Matthew 8:28)

### HEALING
- **Meaning:** "Divine restoration, Christ's compassion"
- **Context:** The healing of the woman with the issue of blood through her faith
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's compassion and His response to persistent faith
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual healing (Matthew 9:20)

### RESURRECTION
- **Meaning:** "Divine life, Christ's victory"
- **Context:** The raising of Jairus' daughter from death demonstrating Christ's power over death
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's victory and His authority over life and death
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual resurrection (Luke 8:41)

### FAITH
- **Meaning:** "Divine trust, Christ's response"
- **Context:** The faith of the woman with the issue of blood and Jairus' faith for his daughter
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's response and His reward for persistent faith
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual faith (Matthew 9:22)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "My name is Legion: for we are many"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any multiple personality or possession
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's power - The legion represents the multitude of demons that possessed the man, showing Christ's power over overwhelming spiritual opposition.

### "Daughter, thy faith hath made thee whole"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any healing or faith
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's response - The faith represents persistent trust in Christ's power that brings healing and restoration.
