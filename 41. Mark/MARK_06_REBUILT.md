## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "A prophet is not without honour, but in his own country, and among his own kin, and in his own house"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any honor or recognition
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's suffering - The lack of honor represents Christ's experience of rejection by those who should have known Him best, showing His identification with suffering prophets.

### "Be of good cheer: it is I; be not afraid"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any comfort or reassurance
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's authority - The comfort represents Christ's complete authority over creation and His power to bring peace in the midst of storms.
