## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### TRIUMPH
- **Meaning:** "Divine kingship, Christ's entry"
- **Context:** Christ's triumphal entry into Jerusalem as the promised King and Messiah
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's kingship and His fulfillment of messianic prophecy
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual triumph (Matthew 21:1)

### PURIFICATION
- **Meaning:** "Divine holiness, Christ's cleansing"
- **Context:** Christ's cleansing of the temple from commercial corruption and idolatry
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's holiness and His zeal for pure worship
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual purification (John 2:16)

### JUDGMENT
- **Meaning:** "Divine justice, Christ's authority"
- **Context:** Christ's judgment on the fruitless fig tree representing spiritual barrenness
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's authority and His power over spiritual barrenness
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual judgment (Matthew 21:19)

### FAITH
- **Meaning:** "Divine trust, Christ's power"
- **Context:** Christ's teaching on faith that can move mountains and the importance of forgiveness
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's power and His requirement for faith in prayer
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual faith (Matthew 17:20)

2 And saith unto them, Go your way into the village over against you: and as soon as ye be entered into it, ye shall find a colt tied, whereon never man sat; loose him, and bring him.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "My house shall be called of all nations the house of prayer; but ye have made it a den of thieves"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any church or religious building
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's temple - The house represents God's dwelling place meant for prayer, which had been corrupted by commercial exploitation.

### "Have faith in God"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any belief or confidence
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's power - The faith represents trust in God's power that can accomplish the impossible, including moving mountains.
