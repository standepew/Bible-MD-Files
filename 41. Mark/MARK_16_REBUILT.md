1 And when the sabbath was past, Mary Magdalene, and Mary the mother of James, and Salome, had bought sweet spices, that they might come and anoint him.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "He that believeth and is baptized shall be saved; but he that believeth not shall be damned"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any belief or ritual
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's salvation - The belief represents genuine faith in Christ, and baptism represents obedience to His commands, both necessary for salvation.

### "Go ye into all the world, and preach the gospel to every creature"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any preaching or evangelism
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's mission - The preaching represents the proclamation of the good news of Christ's salvation to all people everywhere.
