## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### HUMILITY
- **Meaning:** "Divine condescension, Christ's incarnation"
- **Context:** Christ's birth in a manger among humble shepherds, showing His condescension
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's incarnation and His identification with the lowly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual humility (Philippians 2:7)

### REVELATION
- **Meaning:** "Divine disclosure, Christ's manifestation"
- **Context:** The angelic revelation to shepherds and Simeon's recognition of Christ as salvation
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's manifestation and His revelation to all people
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual revelation (John 1:14)

### WISDOM
- **Meaning:** "Divine knowledge, Christ's understanding"
- **Context:** Christ's wisdom demonstrated at age 12 in the temple with the doctors
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's understanding and His divine wisdom from childhood
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual wisdom (Colossians 2:3)

### OBEDIENCE
- **Meaning:** "Divine submission, Christ's example"
- **Context:** Christ's obedience to His earthly parents and His Father's business
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's example and His perfect obedience to divine and earthly authority
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual obedience (Hebrews 5:8)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Glory to God in the highest, and on earth peace, good will toward men"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any peace or goodwill
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's incarnation - The peace represents the reconciliation between God and man made possible through the birth of the Messiah.

### "I must be about my Father's business"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any work or ministry
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's identity - The Father's business represents Christ's divine mission and His unique relationship with the Father.

---

## SYMBOLIC THREADS

### Manger as Spiritual Humility
- **Physical manger:** Laid manger (Luke 2:9)
* **Spiritual manger:* Spiritual humility (Isaiah 1:3)
* **Christ's manger:* Perfect humility (John 10:11)
* **Eternal manger:* Complete condescension (Revelation 22)

### Swaddling Clothes as Spiritual Protection
- **Physical clothes:** Swaddling clothes (Luke 2:9)
* **Spiritual clothes:* Spiritual protection (Ezekiel 16:4)
* **Christ's clothes:* Perfect protection (Matthew 27:59)
* **Eternal clothes:* Complete preservation (Revelation 22)

### Temple as Spiritual Worship
- **Physical temple:** Present Lord (Luke 2:23)
* **Spiritual temple:* Spiritual worship (1 Corinthians 3:16)
* **Christ's temple:* Perfect worship (John 2:19)
* **Eternal temple:* Complete presence (Revelation 22)

### Sword as Spiritual Suffering
- **Physical sword:** Sword pierce (Luke 2:35)
* **Spiritual sword:* Spiritual suffering (Hebrews 4:12)
* **Christ's sword:* Perfect suffering (John 19:34)
* **Eternal sword:* Complete redemption (Revelation 22)
