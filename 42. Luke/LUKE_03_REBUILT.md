## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### REPENTANCE
- **Meaning:** "Divine transformation, Christ's preparation"
- **Context:** John's baptism of repentance preparing the way for Christ's ministry
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's preparation and His call for spiritual transformation
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual repentance (Matthew 3:2)

### BAPTISM
- **Meaning:** "Divine identification, Christ's example"
- **Context:** John's baptism with water and Christ's baptism with the Holy Spirit
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's example and His identification with humanity
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual baptism (Matthew 3:16)

### AUTHORITY
- **Meaning:** "Divine power, Christ's supremacy"
- **Context:** John's recognition of Christ's superior authority and power
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's supremacy and His authority over all
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual authority (John 1:27)

### LINEAGE
- **Meaning:** "Divine fulfillment, Christ's humanity"
- **Context:** Christ's genealogy tracing back to Adam and God, showing His divine and human nature
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's humanity and His fulfillment of divine promises
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual lineage (Matthew 1:1)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "I indeed baptize you with water; but one mightier than I cometh, the latchet of whose shoes I am not worthy to unloose: he shall baptize you with the Holy Ghost and with fire"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any baptism or religious ritual
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's supremacy - The water baptism represents John's preparatory ministry, while the Holy Ghost and fire baptism represent Christ's superior authority and power.

### "Bring forth therefore fruits worthy of repentance"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any good works or moral behavior
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's requirement - The fruits represent the evidence of genuine repentance and transformation, not the cause of salvation.

## SYMBOLIC THREADS

### Water as Spiritual Cleansing
- **Physical water:** Baptism repentance (Luke 3:6)
* **Spiritual water:* Spiritual cleansing (Ezekiel 36:25)
* **Christ's water:* Perfect cleansing (John 3:5)
* **Eternal water:* Complete purification (Revelation 22)

### Fire as Spiritual Judgment
- **Physical fire:** Chaff fire (Luke 3:20)
* **Spiritual fire:* Spiritual judgment (Malachi 4:1)
* **Christ's fire:* Perfect judgment (Matthew 3:12)
* **Eternal fire:* Complete purification (Revelation 22)

### Dove as Spiritual Purity
- **Physical dove:** Bodily shape (Luke 3:24)
* **Spiritual dove:* Spiritual purity (Genesis 8:8)
* **Christ's dove:* Perfect purity (Matthew 3:16)
* **Eternal dove:* Complete holiness (Revelation 22)

### Tree as Spiritual Life
- **Physical tree:** Good fruit (Luke 3:12)
* **Spiritual tree:* Spiritual life (Psalm 1:3)
* **Christ's tree:* Perfect life (John 15:5)
* **Eternal tree:* Complete nourishment (Revelation 22)
