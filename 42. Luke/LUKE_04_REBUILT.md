## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Man shall not live by bread alone, but by every word of God"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any spiritual nourishment or Bible study
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's obedience - The word of God represents Christ's complete trust in and obedience to God's word over physical sustenance.

### "No prophet is accepted in his own country"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any rejection or lack of recognition
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's experience - The rejection represents Christ's fulfillment of prophecy and His experience of being rejected by His own people.
