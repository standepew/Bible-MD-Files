## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### DISCIPLESHIP
- **Meaning:** "Divine following, Christ's teaching"
- **Context:** The parable of the sower explaining different responses to Christ's teaching
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's teaching and His call for fruitful discipleship
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual discipleship (Matthew 13:3)

### FAITH
- **Meaning:** "Divine trust, Christ's power"
- **Context:** The calming of the storm and healing miracles demonstrating Christ's power over nature and disease
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's power and His response to genuine faith
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual faith (Matthew 8:26)

### AUTHORITY
- **Meaning:** "Divine supremacy, Christ's dominion"
- **Context:** Christ's authority over demons, storms, disease, and death
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's supremacy and His dominion over all creation
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual authority (Mark 4:39)

### RESURRECTION
- **Meaning:** "Divine life, Christ's victory"
- **Context:** The raising of Jairus' daughter demonstrating Christ's power over death
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's victory and His power to give life
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual resurrection (John 11:25)

4 And when much people were gathered together, and were come to him out of every city, he spake by a parable:  

5 A sower went out to sow his seed: and as he sowed, some fell by the way side; and it was trodden down, and the fowls of the air devoured it.

7 And some fell among thorns; and the thorns sprang up with it, and choked it.

15 But that on the good ground are they, which in an honest and good heart, having heard the word, keep it, and bring forth fruit with patience.

18 Take heed therefore how ye hear: for whosoever hath, to him shall be given; and whosoever hath not, from him shall be taken even that which he seemeth to have.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "He that hath ears to hear, let him hear"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any listening or attention
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's teaching - The hearing represents spiritual understanding and receptivity to Christ's word.

### "My mother and my brethren are these which hear the word of God, and do it"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any family or community
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's spiritual family - The true family consists of those who hear and obey God's word, not biological relationships.
