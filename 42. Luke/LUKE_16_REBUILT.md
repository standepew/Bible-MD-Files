- **Significance:** Model of Christ's calling and His teaching on using resources for eternal purposes

- **Meaning:** "Divine reliability, Christ's requirement"

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Ye cannot serve God and mammon"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any balance of priorities or wealth management
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's exclusivity - The service represents total devotion, and mammon represents materialism that competes with God for supreme allegiance.

### "They have Moses and the prophets; let them hear them"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any religious instruction or moral teaching
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's revelation - Moses and the prophets represent the complete revelation of God sufficient for salvation, apart from spectacular signs.
