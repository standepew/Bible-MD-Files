## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Heaven and earth shall pass away: but my words shall not pass away"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any enduring statements or human wisdom
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's divinity - The words represent Christ's divine revelation, which is more enduring than creation itself.

### "Watch ye therefore, and pray always"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any vigilance or spiritual discipline
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's return - The watching and praying represent readiness for Christ's second coming, not general spiritual practices.
