## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### BETRAYAL
- **Meaning:** "Divine treason, Christ's suffering"
- **Context:** Judas's betrayal of Christ for money, fulfilling prophecy
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's suffering and the cost of human treachery
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual betrayal (2 Timothy 3:4)

### SACRIFICE
- **Meaning:** "Divine offering, Christ's atonement"
- **Context:** Christ's institution of the Lord's Supper, His body and blood given
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's atonement and the foundation of the new covenant
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual sacrifice (Hebrews 10:12)

### SERVICE
- **Meaning:** "Divine humility, Christ's example"
- **Context:** Christ's teaching on greatness through service, exemplified by Himself
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's example and His call for servant leadership

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "This is my body which is given for you: this do in remembrance of me"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any memorial service or symbolic ritual
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's atonement - The body represents Christ's actual sacrifice, and the remembrance is participation in His death.

### "Not my will, but thine, be done"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any submission to authority or divine will
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's obedience - The will represents Christ's complete submission to the Father's redemptive plan.
