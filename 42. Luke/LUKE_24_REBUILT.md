## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Why seek ye the living among the dead?"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any searching for life or meaning
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's resurrection - The living represents the risen Christ, and the dead represents the tomb where He no longer is.

### "Did not our heart burn within us, while he talked with us by the way?"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any emotional experience or spiritual feeling
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's revelation - The heart burn represents the spiritual illumination when Christ opens the Scriptures.
