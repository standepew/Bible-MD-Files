## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### WORD
- **Meaning:** "Divine Logos, Christ's person"
- **Context:** The eternal Word who was with God and was God, who became flesh
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's divinity and His incarnation for redemption
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual Word (Revelation 19:13)

### LIGHT
- **Meaning:** "Divine illumination, Christ's revelation"
- **Context:** Christ as the true Light that enlightens every man and shines in darkness
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's revelation and His victory over spiritual darkness
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual light (John 8:12)

### LAMB
- **Meaning:** "Divine sacrifice, Christ's atonement"
- **Context:** Christ as the Lamb of God who takes away the sin of the world
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's atonement and His fulfillment of sacrificial typology
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual Lamb (1 Peter 1:19)

### SON OF GOD
- **Meaning:** "Divine sonship, Christ's identity"
- **Context:** Christ's identity as the only begotten Son who declares the Father
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's unique relationship with the Father and His divine nature
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual Sonship (Romans 8:29)

- **Physical Word:** Beginning Word (John 1:3)
* **Spiritual Word:* Christ's incarnation (Genesis 1:1)
* **Christ's Word:* Perfect incarnation (Colossians 1:16)
* **Eternal Word:* Complete revelation (Revelation 22)
