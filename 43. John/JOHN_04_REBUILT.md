## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### LIVING WATER
- **Meaning:** "Divine satisfaction, Christ's salvation"
- **Context:** Christ's offer of spiritual water that satisfies eternally, contrasting with physical water
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's salvation and His provision of eternal satisfaction
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual satisfaction (Revelation 22:17)

### TRUE WORSHIP
- **Meaning:** "Divine acceptance, Christ's presence"
- **Context:** Worship in spirit and truth, not limited to location or ritual
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's presence and His requirement for genuine worship
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual worship (Philippians 3:3)

### HARVEST
- **Meaning:** "Divine mission, Christ's calling"
- **Context:** The spiritual harvest ready for gathering, Christ's mission to save souls
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's calling and His urgency in evangelism
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual harvest (Matthew 9:37)

### FAITH
- **Meaning:** "Divine trust, Christ's requirement"
- **Context:** The nobleman's faith in Christ's word without seeing physical evidence
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's requirement and His response to genuine faith
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual faith (Hebrews 11:1)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "God is a Spirit: and they that worship him must worship him in spirit and in truth"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any spiritual experience or emotional worship
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's requirement - The spirit and truth represent worship that aligns with God's nature and revelation.

### "My meat is to do the will of him that sent me, and to finish his work"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any service work or humanitarian effort
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's mission - The meat represents Christ's complete dedication to fulfilling the Father's redemptive plan.

* **Spiritual water:* Spiritual satisfaction (Jeremiah 2:13)
* **Christ's water:* Perfect satisfaction (Isaiah 12:3)
* **Eternal water:* Complete fulfillment (Revelation 22)

### Mountain as Spiritual Worship
- **Physical mountain:** Mountain Jerusalem (John 4:20)
* **Spiritual mountain:* Spiritual worship (Deuteronomy 12:5)
* **Christ's mountain:* Perfect worship (Hebrews 12:18)
* **Eternal mountain:* Complete presence (Revelation 22)

### Harvest as Spiritual Mission
- **Physical harvest:** White harvest (John 4:35)
* **Spiritual harvest:* Spiritual mission (1 Corinthians 3:6)
* **Christ's harvest:* Perfect mission (Galatians 6:9)
* **Eternal harvest:* Complete gathering (Revelation 22)
