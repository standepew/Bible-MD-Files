## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Judge not according to the appearance, but judge righteous judgment"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any human judgment or opinion
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's divine judgment - The righteous judgment represents God's standard of justice, not human preference.

### "If any man thirst, let him come unto me, and drink"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any physical thirst or material need
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's spiritual provision - The thirst represents spiritual longing that only Christ can satisfy.
