## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### LIGHT OF THE WORLD
- **Meaning:** "Divine illumination, Christ's revelation"
- **Context:** Christ as the light that dispels spiritual darkness and gives life
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's revelation and His victory over spiritual darkness
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual light (Ephesians 5:8)

### TRUTH
- **Meaning:** "Divine reality, Christ's freedom"
- **Context:** Christ's truth that brings freedom from sin and spiritual bondage
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's freedom and His liberation from spiritual slavery
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual truth (John 14:6)

### FREEDOM
- **Meaning:** "Divine liberty, Christ's salvation"
- **Context:** Freedom from sin through Christ, contrasting with worldly bondage
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's salvation and His deliverance from spiritual slavery
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual freedom (Galatians 5:1)

### I AM
- **Meaning:** "Divine existence, Christ's deity"
- **Context:** Christ's eternal existence before Abraham, demonstrating His divine nature
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's deity and His eternal relationship with the Father
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual deity (Colossians 2:9)
