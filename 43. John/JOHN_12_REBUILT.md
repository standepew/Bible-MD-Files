## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### ANOINTING
- **Meaning:** "Divine preparation, Christ's burial"
- **Context:** Mary's anointing of Jesus's feet, preparing Him for burial and demonstrating devotion
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's preparation and His worthiness of sacrificial devotion
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual preparation (1 Peter 3:15)

### TRIUMPHAL ENTRY
- **Meaning:** "Divine kingship, Christ's Messiahship"
- **Context:** Christ's entry into Jerusalem as King, fulfilling Messianic prophecy
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's kingship and His fulfillment of Old Testament prophecy
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual kingship (Revelation 19:16)

### CORN OF WHEAT
- **Meaning:** "Divine sacrifice, Christ's death"
- **Context:** The grain that must die to bring forth fruit, representing Christ's sacrificial death
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's sacrifice and His death bringing spiritual harvest
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual sacrifice (1 Corinthians 15:36)

### UNBELIEF
- **Meaning:** "Divine judgment, Christ's rejection"
- **Context:** The fulfillment of Isaiah's prophecy about people's rejection of Christ despite miracles
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's rejection and His fulfillment of prophetic scripture
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual rejection (2 Thessalonians 2:12)

## PROPHETICAL/TYPOLOGICAL CONNECTIONS

### Mary Anoints Jesus as Type of Christ's Preparation
- **Physical anointing:** Mary anointed (John 12:3)
* **Spiritual anointing:* Christ's preparation (Matthew 26:7)
* **Christ's anointing:* Perfect preparation (Mark 14:3)
* **Eternal anointing:* Complete dedication (Revelation 22)

### The Triumphal Entry as Type of Christ's Kingship
- **Physical entry:** Triumphal entry (John 12:14)
* **Spiritual entry:* Christ's kingship (Zechariah 9:9)
* **Christ's entry:* Perfect kingship (Psalm 118:25)
* **Eternal entry:* Complete reign (Revelation 22)

### Greeks Seek Jesus as Type of Christ's Universal Mission
- **Physical Greeks:** Greeks worship (John 12:22)
* **Spiritual Greeks:* Christ's universal mission (Acts 8:27)
* **Christ's Greeks:* Perfect universal mission (Romans 1:14)
* **Eternal Greeks:* Complete worldwide reach (Revelation 22)

### The Son of Man Must Be Lifted Up as Type of Christ's Sacrifice
- **Physical lifting:** Lifted earth (John 12:32)
* **Spiritual lifting:* Christ's sacrifice (John 14:30)
* **Christ's lifting:* Perfect sacrifice (Ephesians 2:2)
* **Eternal lifting:* Complete redemption (Revelation 22)

### "Except a corn of wheat fall into the ground and die, it abideth alone: but if it die, it bringeth forth much fruit"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any agricultural principle or natural process
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's sacrificial death - The grain represents Christ's death that brings spiritual harvest.

### "The prince of this world be cast out"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any worldly power or political authority
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's victory over Satan - The prince represents the devil and his ultimate defeat.

## SYMBOLIC THREADS

### Ointment as Spiritual Devotion
- **Physical ointment:** Spikenard ointment (John 12:3)
* **Spiritual ointment:* Spiritual devotion (Matthew 26:7)
* **Christ's ointment:* Perfect devotion (Mark 14:3)
* **Eternal ointment:* Complete dedication (Revelation 22)

### Palm Branches as Spiritual Victory
- **Physical branches:** Palm trees (John 12:14)
* **Spiritual branches:* Spiritual victory (Revelation 7:9)
* **Christ's branches:* Perfect victory (Zechariah 9:9)
* **Eternal branches:* Complete triumph (Revelation 22)

### Wheat as Spiritual Harvest
- **Physical wheat:** Corn wheat (John 12:24)
* **Spiritual wheat:* Spiritual harvest (1 Corinthians 15:36)
* **Christ's wheat:* Perfect harvest (Matthew 13:25)
* **Eternal wheat:* Complete ingathering (Revelation 22)

### Voice as Spiritual Authority
- **Physical voice:** Voice heaven (John 12:30)
* **Spiritual voice:* Spiritual authority (Acts 2:6)
* **Christ's voice:* Perfect authority (2 Peter 1:17)
* **Eternal voice:* Complete dominion (Revelation 22)
