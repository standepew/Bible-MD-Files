## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "A new commandment I give unto you, That ye love one another; as I have loved you, that ye also love one another"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any human affection or sentimental love
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's sacrificial love - The love represents Christ's selfless example and His call to believers to love as He loved.

### "If I wash thee not, thou hast no part with me"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any ritual washing or physical cleansing
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's spiritual cleansing - The washing represents the necessity of Christ's cleansing for spiritual fellowship.
