## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### MANSIONS
- **Meaning:** "Divine dwelling, Christ's preparation"
- **Context:** Christ's preparation of eternal dwelling places for believers in the Father's house
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's preparation and His provision of eternal security
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual dwelling (2 Corinthians 5:1)

### WAY
- **Meaning:** "Divine access, Christ's exclusivity"
- **Context:** Christ as the exclusive way to the Father, demonstrating His unique role
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's exclusivity and His role as sole mediator
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual access (Hebrews 10:20)

### COMFORTER
- **Meaning:** "Divine presence, Christ's provision"
- **Context:** The Holy Spirit as Christ's provision for believers' ongoing comfort and guidance
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's provision and His sending of the Spirit
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual comfort (2 Corinthians 1:3)

### PEACE
- **Meaning:** "Divine tranquility, Christ's gift"
- **Context:** Christ's peace that differs from worldly peace, providing true tranquility
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's gift and His provision of spiritual peace
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual peace (Philippians 4:7)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "In my Father's house are many mansions"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any physical buildings or earthly dwellings
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's eternal provision - The mansions represent Christ's preparation of eternal dwelling places for believers.

### "I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any path or philosophy
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's exclusive role - The way represents Christ's unique and sole access to the Father.
