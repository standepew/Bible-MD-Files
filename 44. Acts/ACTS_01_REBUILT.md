## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### ASCENSION
- **Meaning:** "Divine exaltation, Christ's glorification"
- **Context:** Christ's ascension into heaven, demonstrating His glorification and return to the Father
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's glorification and His position at the Father's right hand
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual exaltation (Ephesians 1:20)

### HOLY SPIRIT
- **Meaning:** "Divine presence, Christ's provision"
- **Context:** The promise of the Holy Spirit, demonstrating Christ's provision for believers
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's provision and His sending of divine presence
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual presence (John 14:26)

### WITNESS
- **Meaning:** "Divine testimony, Christ's commission"
- **Context:** The call to be witnesses, demonstrating Christ's commission for evangelism
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's commission and His call to testify of Him
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual testimony (Revelation 12:11)

### APOSTLESHIP
- **Meaning:** "Divine calling, Christ's authority"
- **Context:** The selection of Matthias, demonstrating Christ's authority in calling leaders
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's authority and His establishment of spiritual leadership
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual calling (Ephesians 4:11)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Ye shall receive power, after that the Holy Ghost is come upon you: and ye shall be witnesses unto me both in Jerusalem, and in all Judæa, and in Samaria, and unto the uttermost part of the earth"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any human power or personal strength
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's divine power - The power represents the Holy Spirit's divine enablement for witness.

### "It is not for you to know the times or the seasons, which the Father hath put in his own power"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any ignorance or lack of knowledge
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's divine sovereignty - The times represent God's sovereign timing and authority.
