## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### PENTECOST
- **Meaning:** "Divine outpouring, Christ's provision"
- **Context:** The outpouring of the Holy Spirit, demonstrating Christ's provision for the church
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's provision and His establishment of the church age
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual outpouring (1 Corinthians 12:13)

### TONGUES
- **Meaning:** "Divine communication, Christ's empowerment"
- **Context:** Speaking in tongues, demonstrating Christ's empowerment for witness
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's empowerment and His provision for evangelism
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual communication (1 Corinthians 14:2)

### RESURRECTION
- **Meaning:** "Divine victory, Christ's triumph"
- **Context:** Christ's resurrection, demonstrating His victory over death
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's victory and His provision of eternal life
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual resurrection (Romans 6:4)

### CHURCH
- **Meaning:** "Divine community, Christ's body"
- **Context:** The early church fellowship, demonstrating Christ's body on earth
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's body and His establishment of spiritual community
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual community (Ephesians 1:22)

## PROPHETIC/TYPOLOGICAL CONNECTIONS

### The Day of Pentecost as Type of Christ's Provision
- **Physical Pentecost:** Day Pentecost (Acts 2:3)
* **Spiritual Pentecost:* Christ's provision (Leviticus 23:15)
* **Christ's Pentecost:* Perfect provision (Exodus 20:18)
* **Eternal Pentecost:* Complete outpouring (Revelation 22)

### Peter's Sermon as Type of Christ's Testimony
