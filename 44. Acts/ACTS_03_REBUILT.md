## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Silver and gold have I none; but such as I have give I thee: In the name of Jesus Christ of Nazareth rise up and walk"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any poverty or lack of material wealth
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's spiritual wealth - The silver and gold represent material poverty contrasted with Christ's spiritual power to heal.

### "Repent ye therefore, and be converted, that your sins may be blotted out, when the times of refreshing shall come from the presence of the Lord"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any sorrow or regret without heart transformation
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's salvation requirements - The repentance represents genuine heart transformation leading to divine forgiveness.
