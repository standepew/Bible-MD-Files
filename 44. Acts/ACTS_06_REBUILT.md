## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "It is not reason that we should leave the word of God, and serve tables"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any division between spiritual and practical ministry
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's priority of ministry - The serving represents Christ's call to prioritize spiritual ministry while not neglecting practical needs.

### "They were not able to resist the wisdom and the spirit by which he spake"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any human intelligence or eloquence
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's divine wisdom - The wisdom represents Christ's supernatural insight that cannot be resisted by human opposition.
