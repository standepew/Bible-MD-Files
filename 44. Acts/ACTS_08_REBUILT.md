## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Thy money perish with thee, because thou hast thought that the gift of God may be purchased with money"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any wealth or prosperity in general
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's free salvation - The money represents human attempts to purchase what God freely gives through grace.

### "If thou believest with all thine heart, thou mayest"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any intellectual assent or mental agreement
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's requirement for genuine faith - The heart represents complete trust and commitment to Christ.
