## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "I am clean: from henceforth I will go unto the Gentiles"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any personal righteousness or ethnic preference
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's universal mission - The clean represents being free from the blood of those who reject Christ and turning to fulfill His mission to all nations.

### "I will be no judge of such matters"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any religious tolerance or secular neutrality
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's kingdom being spiritual - The matters represent religious disputes that civil authorities should not judge, pointing to Christ's spiritual kingdom.
