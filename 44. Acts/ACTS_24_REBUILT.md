## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "I do the more cheerfully answer for myself"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any personal confidence or legal skill
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's confidence in truth - The cheerfulness represents Paul's confidence in Christ's truth and his trust in God's sovereignty.

### "Go thy way for this time; when I have a convenient season, I will call for thee"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any procrastination or casual postponement
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's call to immediate repentance - The convenient season represents the dangerous delay that leads to spiritual ruin.
