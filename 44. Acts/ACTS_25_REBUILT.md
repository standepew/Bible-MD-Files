## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "I stand at Caesar's judgment seat, where I ought to be judged"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any legal rights or citizenship privileges
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's higher authority - The judgment seat represents Christ's submission to earthly authority while maintaining His higher allegiance to God's kingdom.

### "I appeal unto Caesar"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any legal appeal or human justice system
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's divine providence - The appeal represents God's sovereign plan to take Paul to Rome for His purposes.
