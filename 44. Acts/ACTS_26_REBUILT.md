## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Almost thou persuadest me to be a Christian"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any near conversion or partial faith
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's call to complete commitment - The almost represents the dangerous position of being close to salvation but not fully committed.

### "I am not mad, most noble Festus; but speak forth the words of truth and soberness"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any intellectual defense or rational argument
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's wisdom that appears foolish to the world - The truth represents Christ's wisdom that seems madness to those without spiritual understanding.
