## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### PROVIDENCE
- **Meaning:** "Divine guidance, Christ's sovereignty"
- **Context:** God's sovereign control over the storm and shipwreck, demonstrating Christ's lordship
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's sovereignty and His control over all circumstances
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual providence (Romans 8:28)

### ASSURANCE
- **Meaning:** "Divine confidence, Christ's promise"
- **Context:** Paul's assurance of safety through God's promise, demonstrating Christ's faithfulness
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's faithfulness and His promise of protection
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual assurance (Hebrews 10:35)

### LEADERSHIP
- **Meaning:** "Divine authority, Christ's guidance"
- **Context:** Paul's leadership during the crisis, demonstrating Christ's wisdom and guidance
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's leadership and His wisdom in crisis situations
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual leadership (1 Peter 5:3)

### DELIVERANCE
- **Meaning:** "Divine rescue, Christ's salvation"
- **Context:** The miraculous deliverance from shipwreck, demonstrating Christ's saving power
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's salvation and His power to deliver from danger
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual deliverance (Colossians 1:13)

### "Except these abide in the ship, ye cannot be saved"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any physical vessel or means of salvation
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's means of salvation - The ship represents the community of faith through which God provides salvation.
