## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "No doubt this man is a murderer, whom, though he hath escaped the sea, yet vengeance suffereth not to live"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any human judgment or superstition
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's victory over false accusation - The vengeance represents human misunderstanding of divine justice, which Christ overcomes through His power.

### "Be it known therefore unto you, that the salvation of God is sent unto the Gentiles, and that they will hear it"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any religious exclusivity or ethnic preference
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's universal salvation - The Gentiles represent God's inclusive plan of salvation for all nations through Christ.
