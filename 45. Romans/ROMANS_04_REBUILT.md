## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Abraham believed God, and it was counted unto him for righteousness"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any religious belief or human faithfulness
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's righteousness - The faith represents trust in God's promise fulfilled in Christ, not human religious effort.

### "The law worketh wrath: for where no law is, there is no transgression"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any legal system or moral code
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's fulfillment of the law - The law represents the standard that reveals our need for Christ's righteousness.
