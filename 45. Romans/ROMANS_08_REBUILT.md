## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### CONDEMNATION
- **Meaning:** "Divine judgment, Christ's salvation"
- **Context:** No condemnation for those in Christ Jesus, demonstrating Christ's complete salvation
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's salvation and His complete deliverance from judgment
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual condemnation (John 5:24)

### SPIRIT
- **Meaning:** "Divine presence, Christ's indwelling"
- **Context:** Life in the Spirit through Christ's indwelling, demonstrating Christ's abiding presence
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's presence and His indwelling power in believers
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual Spirit (1 Corinthians 6:19)

### ADOPTION
- **Meaning:** "Divine sonship, Christ's relationship"
- **Context:** Adoption as children of God through the Spirit, demonstrating Christ's familial relationship
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's relationship and His invitation to divine sonship
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual adoption (Galatians 4:6)

### VICTORY
- **Meaning:** "Divine triumph, Christ's conquest"
- **Context:** More than conquerors through Christ's love, demonstrating Christ's victorious power
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's victory and His triumphant power over all circumstances
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual victory (1 John 5:4)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "All things work together for good"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any positive outcome or human success
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's sovereign purpose - The working together represents God's redemptive purpose in Christ for those who love Him.

### "More than conquerors"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any human achievement or personal victory
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's victory through suffering - The conquering represents Christ's triumph that works through our trials and sufferings.
