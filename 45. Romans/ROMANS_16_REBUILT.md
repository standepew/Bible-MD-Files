### SERVICE
- **Meaning:** "Divine ministry, Christ's work"
- **Context:** Service in the church and ministry to Christ, demonstrating Christ's work through believers
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's work and His call to faithful service
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual service (1 Timothy 3:11)

- **Meaning:** "Divine partnership, Christ's community"
- **Context:** Fellowship among believers and workers in Christ, demonstrating Christ's community
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's community and His power to unite believers
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual fellowship (Acts 9:36)

- **Context:** Labor in the Lord and ministry for Christ, demonstrating Christ's work through believers

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Mark them which cause divisions and offences"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any disagreement or church conflict
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's doctrinal purity - The divisions represent false teaching contrary to apostolic doctrine, not mere differences of opinion.

### "The God of peace shall bruise Satan under your feet"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any human victory or success
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's ultimate victory - The bruising represents Christ's complete triumph over Satan through the gospel.
