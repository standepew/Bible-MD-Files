## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### CARNAL
- **Meaning:** "Spiritual immaturity, Christ's growth"
- **Context:** Carnal Christians as babes needing milk, demonstrating Christ's call to spiritual maturity
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's growth and His power to mature believers
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual carnality (Hebrews 5:12)

### FOUNDATION
- **Meaning:** "Spiritual basis, Christ's person"
- **Context:** Christ as the only foundation for God's building, demonstrating Christ's foundational role
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's foundation and His role as the basis of all spiritual work
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual foundation (Ephesians 2:20)

### TEMPLE
- **Meaning:** "Spiritual dwelling, Christ's presence"
- **Context:** Believers as the temple of God with the Spirit dwelling within, demonstrating Christ's indwelling presence
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's presence and His indwelling in believers
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual temple (1 Corinthians 6:19)

### WISDOM
- **Meaning:** "Divine understanding, Christ's knowledge"
- **Context:** The wisdom of this world as foolishness with God, demonstrating Christ's true wisdom
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's wisdom and His power to give true understanding
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual wisdom (Job 5:13)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "I have fed you with milk, and not with meat"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any physical diet or gradual learning process
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's spiritual nourishment - The milk represents basic spiritual truths for immature believers, not any human education.

### "Ye are God's building"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any construction or human achievement
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's church - The building represents the spiritual temple that God is constructing through Christ and His servants.
