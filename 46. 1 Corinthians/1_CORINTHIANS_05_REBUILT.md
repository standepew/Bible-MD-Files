**Common mispretation:** This is about any small influence or gradual change
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's church purity - The leaven represents sin that corrupts the entire church community, requiring decisive action.

### "Put away from among yourselves that wicked person"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any social exclusion or personal dislike
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's church discipline - The putting away represents church discipline to maintain purity, not personal judgment.
