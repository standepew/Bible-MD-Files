## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "It is better to marry than to burn"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any romantic desire or sexual passion
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's protection - The burning represents uncontrolled sexual passion that leads to sin, not normal desire.

### "The time is short"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any urgency or time pressure
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's return - The short time represents the brief period before Christ's return, calling believers to eternal perspective.
