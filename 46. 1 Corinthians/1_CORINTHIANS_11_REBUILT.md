## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### HEAD
- **Meaning:** "Divine authority, Christ's leadership"
- **Context:** Christ as head of man, man as head of woman, God as head of Christ, demonstrating Christ's authority structure
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's leadership and His divine order of authority
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual head (Ephesians 5:23)

### GLORY
- **Meaning:** "Divine honor, Christ's radiance"
- **Context:** Man as glory of God, woman as glory of man, demonstrating Christ's reflected honor
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's radiance and His power to give honor
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual glory (Colossians 1:18)

### ORDINANCES
- **Meaning:** "Divine commands, Christ's instructions"
- **Context:** Ordinances delivered by Paul and received from Christ, demonstrating Christ's authoritative teaching
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's instructions and His power to guide the church
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual ordinances (Numbers 6:5)

### REMEMBRANCE
- **Meaning:** "Divine memorial, Christ's presence"
- **Context:** The Lord's Supper as remembrance of Christ's death, demonstrating Christ's memorial presence
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's presence and His power to be remembered
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual remembrance (Matthew 26:26)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "The head of the woman is the man"
**Common misinterpretation:** This is about any male dominance or gender superiority
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's divine order - The headship represents Christ's authority structure in the church and family, not human domination.

### "This is my body, which is broken for you"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any physical breaking or suffering
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's sacrificial death - The broken body represents Christ's sacrificial death on the cross for our salvation.
