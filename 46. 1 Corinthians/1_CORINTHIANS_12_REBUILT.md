## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### SPIRITUAL GIFTS
- **Meaning:** "Divine enablements, Christ's powers"
- **Context:** Various gifts given by the Spirit for the church's benefit, demonstrating Christ's empowering presence
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's powers and His ability to equip believers for service
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual gifts (1 John 4:2)

### BODY
- **Meaning:** "Divine unity, Christ's church"
- **Context:** The church as one body with many members, demonstrating Christ's unifying work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's unity and His power to create diverse unity
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual body (Ephesians 4:4)

### UNITY
- **Meaning:** "Divine harmony, Christ's oneness"
- **Context:** Unity in diversity within the body of Christ, demonstrating Christ's harmonizing power
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's oneness and His ability to create harmony from diversity
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual unity (Colossians 3:11)

### ADMINISTRATION
- **Meaning:** "Divine service, Christ's ministry"
- **Context:** Different administrations but the same Lord, demonstrating Christ's varied ministries
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's ministry and His power to work through diverse gifts
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual administration (Romans 12:6)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "There are diversities of gifts, but the same Spirit"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any human talents or abilities
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's spiritual gifts - The diversities represent different spiritual enablements given by the same Holy Spirit for the church's benefit.

### "The body is one, and hath many members"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any organization or group unity
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's church - The body represents the spiritual unity of believers in Christ with diverse functions.
