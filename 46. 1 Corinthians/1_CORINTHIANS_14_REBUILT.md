## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### PROPHECY
- **Meaning:** "Divine revelation, Christ's message"
- **Context:** Prophecy as speaking to men for edification, exhortation, and comfort, demonstrating Christ's revealing work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's message and His power to speak through believers
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual prophecy (Acts 2:4)

### TONGUES
- **Meaning:** "Divine language, Christ's communication"
- **Context:** Tongues as speaking mysteries to God requiring interpretation, demonstrating Christ's supernatural communication
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's communication and His power to speak in supernatural ways
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual tongues (Acts 2:17)

### EDIFICATION
- **Meaning:** "Divine building, Christ's growth"
- **Context:** Edification as building up the church through proper spiritual gifts, demonstrating Christ's building work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's growth and His power to build up believers
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual edification (Ephesians 4:12)

### ORDER
- **Meaning:** "Divine arrangement, Christ's peace"
- **Context:** Order in worship as God's peace rather than confusion, demonstrating Christ's peaceful arrangement
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's peace and His power to bring order to worship
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual order (Colossians 2:19)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "God is not the author of confusion, but of peace"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any disorder or lack of organization
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's church order - The confusion represents disorder in worship, while peace represents Christ's orderly arrangement in the church.

### "Let your women keep silence in the churches"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any female silence or gender discrimination
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's church order - The silence represents proper order in worship according to God's design, not any suppression of women.
