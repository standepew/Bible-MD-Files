## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### RESURRECTION
- **Meaning:** "Divine victory, Christ's triumph"
- **Context:** Christ's resurrection and the resurrection of the dead, demonstrating Christ's victory over death
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's triumph and His power to give eternal life
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual resurrection (Acts 26:8)

### CORRUPTION
- **Meaning:** "Divine decay, Christ's transformation"
- **Context:** Corruption that cannot inherit God's kingdom, demonstrating Christ's transformation from decay
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's transformation and His power to overcome corruption
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual corruption (Daniel 12:2)

### INCORRUPTION
- **Meaning:** "Divine perfection, Christ's holiness"
- **Context:** Incorruption that the dead shall be raised in, demonstrating Christ's perfect holiness
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's holiness and His power to give incorruptible life
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual incorruption (Leviticus 23:10)

### VICTORY
- **Meaning:** "Divine conquest, Christ's triumph"
- **Context:** Victory over death through Christ, demonstrating Christ's complete conquest
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's conquest and His power to give victory over death
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual victory (Hosea 13:14)

## PROPHETICAL/TYPOLOGICAL CONNECTIONS

### The Resurrection of Christ as Type of Christ's Victory
- **Physical victory:** Christ died (1 Corinthians 15:5)
* **Spiritual victory:* Christ's victory (Isaiah 53:5)
* **Christ's victory:* Perfect victory (Psalm 16:10)
* **Eternal victory:* Complete triumph (Revelation 22)

### The Resurrection of the Dead as Type of Christ's Life
- **Physical life:** Firstfruits (1 Corinthians 15:21)
* **Spiritual life:* Christ's life (Leviticus 23:10)
* **Christ's life:* Perfect life (Romans 5:12)
* **Eternal life:* Complete existence (Revelation 22)

### The Spiritual Body as Type of Christ's Transformation
- **Physical transformation:** Sown corruption (1 Corinthians 15:44)
* **Spiritual transformation:* Christ's transformation (Genesis 1:26)
* **Christ's transformation:* Perfect transformation (2 Corinthians 3:18)
* **Eternal transformation:* Complete renewal (Revelation 22)

### Victory Over Death as Type of Christ's Conquest
- **Physical conquest:** Trumpet sound (1 Corinthians 15:53)

### "The sting of death is sin"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any physical pain or suffering
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's victory - The sting represents sin's power to cause death, which Christ overcame through His death and resurrection.
