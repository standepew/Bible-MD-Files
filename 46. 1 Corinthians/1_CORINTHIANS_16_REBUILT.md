## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### COLLECTION
- **Meaning:** "Divine generosity, Christ's provision"
- **Context:** Collection for the saints as organized giving, demonstrating Christ's provision through believers
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's provision and His power to provide through His people
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual collection (Acts 11:29)

- **Meaning:** "Divine generosity, Christ's giving"
- **Context:** Liberality in giving to Jerusalem, demonstrating Christ's generous nature
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's giving and His power to give generously
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual liberality (2 Corinthians 8:13)

### FAITH
- **Meaning:** "Divine trust, Christ's confidence"
- **Context:** Standing fast in the faith as final exhortation, demonstrating Christ's steadfast trust
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's confidence and His power to give unwavering faith
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual faith (Ephesians 6:13)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Watch ye, stand fast in the faith, quit you like men, be strong"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any human courage or strength
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's spiritual warfare - The standing and strength represent Christ's power to stand firm in faith and act with spiritual maturity.

### "Let all your things be done with charity"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any kindness or good deeds
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's divine love - The charity represents Christ's love that should characterize all Christian actions and relationships.
