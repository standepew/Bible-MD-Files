## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### COMFORT
- **Meaning:** "Divine consolation, Christ's compassion"
- **Context:** God's comfort in tribulation that enables believers to comfort others, demonstrating Christ's compassionate presence
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's compassion and His power to comfort in suffering
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual comfort (Romans 15:5)

### TRIBULATION
- **Meaning:** "Divine testing, Christ's suffering"
- **Context:** Tribulation that produces Christ-like suffering and consolation, demonstrating Christ's redemptive suffering
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's suffering and His power to redeem trials
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual tribulation (Colossians 1:24)

### SINCERITY
- **Meaning:** "Divine purity, Christ's authenticity"
- **Context:** Godly sincerity in contrast to fleshly wisdom, demonstrating Christ's authentic ministry
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's authenticity and His power to produce genuine ministry
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual sincerity (Philippians 3:10)

### PROMISES
- **Meaning:** "Divine assurance, Christ's faithfulness"
- **Context:** God's promises that are "yes" in Christ, demonstrating Christ's complete fulfillment of God's word
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's faithfulness and His power to fulfill all God's promises
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual promises (Romans 4:17)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "God comforteth us in all our tribulation"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any human comfort or relief from suffering
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's divine comfort - The comfort represents God's supernatural consolation that enables believers to comfort others with the same comfort they received from God.

### "All the promises of God in him are yea"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any human promises or assurances
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's complete fulfillment - The promises represent God's divine assurances that find their "yes" and fulfillment in Christ, not human agreements.
