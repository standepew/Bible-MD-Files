- **Significance:** Model of Christ's ambassadorship and His power to minister reconciliation

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "We have this treasure in earthen vessels"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any valuable things in fragile containers
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's incarnation - The earthen vessels represent human bodies containing the divine treasure of the gospel, demonstrating Christ's incarnation.

### "We look not at the things which are seen, but at the things which are not seen"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any imagination or wishful thinking
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's eternal perspective - The unseen things represent eternal spiritual realities that are more real and permanent than temporary physical things.
