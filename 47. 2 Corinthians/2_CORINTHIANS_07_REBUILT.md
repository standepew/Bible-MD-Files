## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### HOLINESS
- **Meaning:** "Divine purity, Christ's sanctification"
- **Context:** Perfecting holiness through cleansing from flesh and spirit, demonstrating Christ's sanctifying work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's sanctification and His power to make believers holy
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual holiness (Leviticus 20:7)

### SORROW
- **Meaning:** "Divine grief, Christ's repentance"
- **Context:** Godly sorrow that works repentance versus worldly sorrow that works death, demonstrating Christ's redemptive grief
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's repentance and His power to transform sorrow into salvation
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual sorrow (Psalm 34:18)

### COMFORT
- **Meaning:** "Divine consolation, Christ's encouragement"
- **Context:** God's comfort in tribulation through Titus's coming, demonstrating Christ's encouraging presence
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's encouragement and His power to comfort in suffering
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual comfort (Isaiah 41:10)

### CONFIDENCE
- **Meaning:** "Divine assurance, Christ's trust"
- **Context:** Confidence in the Corinthians' obedience and spiritual growth, demonstrating Christ's trust in believers
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's trust and His power to give assurance in relationships
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual confidence (Philippians 4:14)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Godly sorrow worketh repentance to salvation"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any feeling bad about mistakes or regret
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's redemptive sorrow - The godly sorrow represents divinely-produced grief over sin that leads to genuine repentance and salvation, not mere emotional regret.

### "Perfecting holiness in the fear of God"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any moral improvement or religious effort
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's sanctifying work - The perfecting represents the ongoing work of the Holy Spirit in making believers holy through reverence for God.
