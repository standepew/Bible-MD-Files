## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### MINISTERING
- **Meaning:** "Divine service, Christ's ministry"
- **Context:** Ministering to the saints through generous giving, demonstrating Christ's serving ministry
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's ministry and His power to serve through believers
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual ministering (Romans 15:26)

### SOWING
- **Meaning:** "Divine planting, Christ's investment"
- **Context:** Sowing bountifully to reap bountifully, demonstrating Christ's investment principle
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's investment and His power to multiply spiritual returns
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual sowing (Proverbs 11:24)

### CHEERFUL
- **Meaning:** "Divine joy, Christ's delight"
- **Context:** Cheerful giving that God loves, demonstrating Christ's joyful generosity
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's delight and His power to give with joy
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual cheerful (Psalm 112:9)

### ABUNDANCE
- **Meaning:** "Divine plenty, Christ's sufficiency"
- **Context:** God's abundant grace that provides sufficiency, demonstrating Christ's abundant provision
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's sufficiency and His power to provide abundantly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual abundance (Philippians 4:19)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "God loveth a cheerful giver"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any positive attitude or happiness
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's joyful generosity - The cheerful giving represents willing, joyful generosity that comes from the heart, not mere external compliance.

### "He which soweth sparingly shall reap also sparingly"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any agricultural or financial investment
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's spiritual principle - The sowing represents spiritual investment in God's kingdom with guaranteed returns according to the investment.
