1 Now I Paul myself beseech you by the meekness and gentleness of Christ, who in presence am base among you, but being absent am bold toward you:

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "The weapons of our warfare are not carnal"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any political or social activism
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's spiritual battle - The weapons represent spiritual power through God to demolish strongholds of false thinking and bring every thought into obedience to Christ.

### "Let him that glorieth, glory in the Lord"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any pride or self-promotion
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's exclusive glory - The glory represents giving all credit and honor to Christ alone, not sharing glory with human achievement.
