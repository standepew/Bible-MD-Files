- **Meaning:** "Divine purity, Christ's innocence"

- **Significance:** Model of Christ's innocence and His power to maintain spiritual purity

- **Significance:** Model of Christ's representatives and His power to expose false messengers

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "I am jealous over you with godly jealousy"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any human jealousy or possessiveness
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's divine protection - The jealousy represents God's protective guardianship over His people, not human envy.

### "Satan himself is transformed into an angel of light"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any deception or trickery
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's spiritual warfare - The transformation represents Satan's ability to appear as something good while being evil, not mere deception.
