## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### BLESSINGS
- **Meaning:** "Divine favor, Christ's grace"
- **Context:** Spiritual blessings in heavenly places in Christ, demonstrating Christ's heavenly favor
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's favor and His power to bless spiritually
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual blessings (John 17:24)

### PREDESTINATED
- **Meaning:** "Divine election, Christ's choice"
- **Context:** Predestination to adoption as children through Christ, demonstrating Christ's sovereign choice
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's choice and His power to elect believers
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual predestinated (Romans 8:30)

### MYSTERY
- **Meaning:** "Divine revelation, Christ's truth"
- **Context:** Mystery of God's will revealed in Christ, demonstrating Christ's revealed truth
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's truth and His power to reveal divine mysteries
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual mystery (Colossians 1:26)

### HEAD
- **Meaning:** "Divine authority, Christ's leadership"
- **Context:** Christ as head over all things to the church, demonstrating Christ's supreme authority
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's authority and His power to lead the church
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual head (Acts 2:32)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Blessed be the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who hath blessed us with all spiritual blessings in heavenly places"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any material blessings or prosperity
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's spiritual blessings - The blessings represent spiritual realities in heavenly places, not earthly prosperity.

### "In whom we have redemption through his blood, the forgiveness of sins"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any forgiveness or pardon
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's sacrificial redemption - The redemption represents being purchased by Christ's blood, not mere forgiveness.
