## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### QUICKENED
- **Meaning:** "Divine life, Christ's resurrection"
- **Context:** Quickened from spiritual death in trespasses and sins, demonstrating Christ's life-giving power
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's resurrection and His power to give spiritual life
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual quickened (Colossians 2:13)

### GRACE
- **Meaning:** "Divine favor, Christ's salvation"
- **Context:** Saved by grace through faith, not of works, demonstrating Christ's unmerited favor
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's salvation and His power to save by grace
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual grace (Romans 5:8)

### WORKMANSHIP
- **Meaning:** "Divine creation, Christ's craftsmanship"
- **Context:** Created as God's workmanship for good works, demonstrating Christ's creative purpose
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's craftsmanship and His power to create for good works
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual workmanship (2 Corinthians 5:17)

### TEMPLE
- **Meaning:** "Divine dwelling, Christ's church"
- **Context:** Built as a holy temple for God's habitation, demonstrating Christ's dwelling place
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's dwelling and His power to inhabit believers
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual temple (1 Peter 2:6)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: it is the gift of God"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any faith or belief system
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's exclusive salvation - The grace represents God's unmerited favor received through faith in Christ, not human effort.

### "We are his workmanship, created in Christ Jesus unto good works"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any moral improvement or good deeds
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's creative purpose - The workmanship represents being created anew in Christ for good works, not human achievement.
