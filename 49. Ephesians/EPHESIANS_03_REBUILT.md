## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### MYSTERY
- **Meaning:** "Divine revelation, Christ's truth"
- **Context:** Mystery of Christ revealed to apostles and prophets, demonstrating Christ's revealed truth
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's truth and His power to reveal divine mysteries
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual mystery (Colossians 1:26)

### FELLOWHEIRS
- **Meaning:** "Divine inheritance, Christ's legacy"
- **Context:** Gentiles as fellowheirs with Jews in Christ, demonstrating Christ's inclusive inheritance
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's inheritance and His power to include all believers
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual fellowheirs (Romans 11:25)

### PRINCIPALITIES
- **Meaning:** "Divine authorities, Christ's dominion"
- **Context:** Principalities and powers knowing God's wisdom through the church, demonstrating Christ's supreme authority
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's authority and His power to rule over all powers
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual principalities (1 Corinthians 2:7)

### INNER
- **Meaning:** "Divine heart, Christ's dwelling"
- **Context:** Inner man strengthened by God's Spirit, demonstrating Christ's indwelling presence
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's dwelling and His power to inhabit the inner being
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual inner (Philippians 4:6)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "That he would grant you, according to the riches of his glory, to be strengthened with might by his Spirit in the inner man"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any personal strength or inner development
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's spiritual strengthening - The inner man represents the spiritual being strengthened by God's Spirit, not human willpower.

### "Now unto him that is able to do exceeding abundantly above all that we ask or think"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any success or achievement
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's divine power - The exceeding abundance represents God's ability to do far beyond human requests or thoughts, not mere success.
