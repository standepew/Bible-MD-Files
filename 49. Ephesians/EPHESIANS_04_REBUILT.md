## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### UNITY
- **Meaning:** "Divine oneness, Christ's body"
- **Context:** Unity of the Spirit in the bond of peace, demonstrating Christ's unified church
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's body and His power to unite believers
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual unity (Colossians 3:12)

### GIFTS
- **Meaning:** "Divine ministry, Christ's service"
- **Context:** Gifts given by ascended Christ for ministry, demonstrating Christ's equipping power
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's service and His power to equip for ministry
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual gifts (Psalm 68:18)

### PERFECT
- **Meaning:** "Divine maturity, Christ's completeness"
- **Context:** Perfect man in the fulness of Christ, demonstrating Christ's mature believers
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's completeness and His power to bring to maturity
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual perfect (Colossians 1:28)

### NEW
- **Meaning:** "Divine transformation, Christ's creation"
- **Context:** New man created in righteousness and holiness, demonstrating Christ's transforming power
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's transformation and His power to create anew
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual new (Romans 12:2)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "One Lord, one faith, one baptism"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any religious unity or denominational agreement
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's essential unity - The seven unities represent the essential oneness in Christ, not human organizational unity.

### "Put off the old man, and put on the new man"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any self-improvement or personality change
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's radical transformation - The old and new man represent the complete change from sin to righteousness in Christ, not behavioral modification.
