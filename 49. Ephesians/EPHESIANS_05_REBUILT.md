## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Walk in love, as Christ also hath loved us, and hath given himself for us"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any love or affection
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's sacrificial love - The love represents Christ's self-giving sacrifice, not mere human affection.

### "Be ye not drunk with wine, wherein is excess; but be filled with the Spirit"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any emotional experience or enthusiasm
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's spiritual fullness - The filling represents being controlled by the Holy Spirit, not emotional excitement.
