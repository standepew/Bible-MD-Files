## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### OBEY
- **Meaning:** "Divine submission, Christ's obedience"
- **Context:** Children obeying parents in the Lord, demonstrating Christ's obedient submission
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's obedience and His power to submit to authority
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual obey (Exodus 20:12)

### ARMOUR
- **Meaning:** "Divine protection, Christ's defense"
- **Context:** Whole armor of God for spiritual warfare, demonstrating Christ's protective power
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's protection and His power to defend believers
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual armour (2 Corinthians 10:4)

### STAND
- **Meaning:** "Divine strength, Christ's steadfastness"
- **Context:** Standing against the devil's wiles with God's armor, demonstrating Christ's steadfast strength
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's steadfastness and His power to resist evil
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual stand (1 Peter 5:8)

### AMBASSADOR
- **Meaning:** "Divine representation, Christ's ministry"
- **Context:** Paul as ambassador in bonds for the gospel, demonstrating Christ's representative ministry
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's representation and His power to send ambassadors
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual ambassador (2 Corinthians 5:20)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Be strong in the Lord, and in the power of his might"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any personal strength or confidence
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's divine strength - The strength represents being empowered by God's might, not human ability.

### "Put on the whole armour of God"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any protection or defense
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's spiritual armor - The armor represents God's spiritual protection for warfare against evil, not physical defense.
