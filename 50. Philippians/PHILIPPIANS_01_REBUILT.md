## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### FELLOWSHIP
- **Meaning:** "Divine partnership, Christ's communion"
- **Context:** Fellowship in the gospel from the first day, demonstrating Christ's partnership in ministry
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's partnership and His power to unite believers in gospel work
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual fellowship (Romans 1:7)

### CONFIDENT
- **Meaning:** "Divine assurance, Christ's certainty"
- **Context:** Confidence that God will complete the good work, demonstrating Christ's faithful completion
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's certainty and His power to complete what He begins
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual confident (1 Thessalonians 5:24)

### BONDS
- **Meaning:** "Divine suffering, Christ's imprisonment"
- **Context:** Bonds in Christ that further the gospel, demonstrating Christ's redemptive suffering
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's suffering and His power to advance the gospel through trials
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual bonds (Acts 28:30)

### LIVE
- **Meaning:** "Divine existence, Christ's life"
- **Context:** To live is Christ and to die is gain, demonstrating Christ's life-giving existence
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's existence and His power to give life worth living
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual live (Galatians 2:20)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "For to me to live is Christ, and to die is gain"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any purpose or meaning in life
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's exclusive Lordship - The living represents existence centered on Christ, not general purpose.

### "I can do all things through Christ which strengtheneth me"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any ability or achievement
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's empowering strength - The strength represents Christ's power for spiritual victory, not human capability.
