## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### REJOICE
- **Meaning:** "Divine joy, Christ's gladness"
- **Context:** Rejoicing in the Lord as opposed to fleshly confidence, demonstrating Christ's spiritual joy
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's joy and His power to give gladness
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual rejoice (Romans 2:29)

### CIRCUMCISION
- **Meaning:** "Divine covenant, Christ's spiritual mark"
- **Context:** True circumcision in spirit versus fleshly concision, demonstrating Christ's spiritual marking
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's marking and His power to create spiritual identity
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual circumcision (Galatians 5:6)

### KNOW
- **Meaning:** "Divine knowledge, Christ's intimate relationship"
- **Context:** Knowing Christ and His resurrection power, demonstrating Christ's revealing knowledge
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's knowledge and His power to reveal Himself
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual know (Matthew 13:44)

### PRESS
- **Meaning:** "Divine pursuit, Christ's striving"
- **Context:** Pressing toward the mark for the prize, demonstrating Christ's determined pursuit
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's pursuit and His power to motivate striving
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual press (1 Corinthians 9:24)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "I press toward the mark for the prize of the high calling of God in Christ Jesus"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any achievement or goal-setting
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's spiritual pursuit - The pressing represents striving for Christ's high calling, not earthly success.

### "For our conversation is in heaven"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any heavenly-mindedness or future focus
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's heavenly citizenship - The conversation represents belonging to Christ's heavenly kingdom, not mere optimism.
