## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### STAND
- **Meaning:** "Divine steadfastness, Christ's firmness"
- **Context:** Standing fast in the Lord as beloved brethren, demonstrating Christ's steadfast faith
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's steadfastness and His power to keep believers firm
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual stand (1 Corinthians 16:13)

### REJOICE
- **Meaning:** "Divine gladness, Christ's joy"
- **Context:** Rejoicing always in the Lord, demonstrating Christ's continual joy
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's joy and His power to give gladness in all circumstances
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual rejoice (1 Thessalonians 5:16)

### PEACE
- **Meaning:** "Divine tranquility, Christ's rest"
- **Context:** Peace of God passing understanding, demonstrating Christ's supernatural peace
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's peace and His power to guard hearts and minds
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual peace (Colossians 3:15)

### CONTENT
- **Meaning:** "Divine satisfaction, Christ's sufficiency"
- **Context:** Content in every state through Christ's strength, demonstrating Christ's sufficient provision
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's contentment and His power to satisfy in all circumstances
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual content (2 Corinthians 11:27)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "I can do all things through Christ which strengtheneth me"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any ability or achievement
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's empowering strength - The strength represents Christ's power for spiritual contentment, not human capability.

### "Be careful for nothing; but in every thing by prayer and supplication with thanksgiving let your requests be made known unto God"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any positive thinking or anxiety management
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's peace through prayer - The carefulness represents freedom from anxiety through Christ's peace, not mere positive thinking.
