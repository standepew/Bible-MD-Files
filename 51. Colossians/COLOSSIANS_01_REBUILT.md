## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### FAITH
- **Meaning:** "Divine trust, Christ's belief"
- **Context:** Faith in Christ Jesus with love to all saints, demonstrating Christ's faithful trust
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's faith and His power to create believing love
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual faith (Ephesians 1:2)

### HOPE
- **Meaning:** "Divine expectation, Christ's anticipation"
- **Context:** Hope laid up in heaven through the gospel, demonstrating Christ's heavenly expectation
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's hope and His power to provide eternal anticipation
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual hope (Romans 8:24)

### KNOWLEDGE
- **Meaning:** "Divine understanding, Christ's wisdom"
- **Context:** Knowledge of God's will in wisdom and spiritual understanding, demonstrating Christ's revealing knowledge
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's knowledge and His power to give divine understanding
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual knowledge (Ephesians 1:17)

### MYSTERY
- **Meaning:** "Divine revelation, Christ's truth"
- **Context:** Mystery hidden but now manifest - Christ in you, demonstrating Christ's revealed truth
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's mystery and His power to reveal hidden truth
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual mystery (Romans 16:25)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "All things were created by him, and for him"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any creation or natural order
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's exclusive creatorship - The creation represents Christ's sovereign work and purpose, not general existence.

### "Christ in you, the hope of glory"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any potential or self-improvement
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's indwelling presence - The hope represents Christ's glorious presence within believers, not human potential.
