- **Meaning:** "Divine struggle, Christ's battle"
- **Context:** Paul's great conflict for believers' spiritual welfare, demonstrating Christ's spiritual warfare
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's battle and His power to fight for His people
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual conflict (1 Thessalonians 3:5)

### FULLNESS
- **Meaning:** "Divine completeness, Christ's totality"
- **Context:** Fullness of Godhead dwelling bodily in Christ, demonstrating Christ's complete deity
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's completeness and His power to contain divine fullness
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual fullness (Ephesians 3:17)

- **Meaning:** "Divine covenant, Christ's spiritual mark"
- **Context:** Circumcision made without hands in Christ, demonstrating Christ's spiritual marking
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's marking and His power to create spiritual identity
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual circumcision (Romans 2:29)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Beware lest any man spoil you through philosophy and vain deceit"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any intellectual pursuit or philosophy
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's exclusive truth - The philosophy represents human wisdom that contradicts Christ, not all learning.

### "Touch not; taste not; handle not"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any rules or restrictions
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's freedom from legalism - The restrictions represent man-made regulations, not biblical principles.
