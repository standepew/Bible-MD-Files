## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### MASTER
- **Meaning:** "Divine authority, Christ's lordship"
- **Context:** Masters having a Master in heaven, demonstrating Christ's supreme authority over all
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's authority and His power to govern justly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual master (Ephesians 6:9)

### PRAYER
- **Meaning:** "Divine communication, Christ's intercession"
- **Context:** Continuing in prayer with thanksgiving, demonstrating Christ's continual intercession
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's prayer and His power to open doors for ministry
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual prayer (1 Corinthians 16:9)

### WISDOM
- **Meaning:** "Divine understanding, Christ's insight"
- **Context:** Walking in wisdom toward outsiders, demonstrating Christ's wise conduct
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's wisdom and His power to redeem time effectively
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual wisdom (Ephesians 5:15)

### MINISTRY
- **Meaning:** "Divine service, Christ's work"
- **Context:** Fulfilling the ministry received in the Lord, demonstrating Christ's faithful service
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's ministry and His power to complete His work
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual ministry (Philemon 1:10)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Let your speech be alway with grace, seasoned with salt"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any polite or tasteful speech
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's gracious communication - The speech represents Christ's words that preserve and purify, not mere politeness.

### "Redeeming the time"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any time management or efficiency
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's eternal perspective - The time represents making the most of opportunities for Christ's kingdom, not productivity.
