## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### FAITH
- **Meaning:** "Divine trust, Christ's belief"
- **Context:** Work of faith with labour of love and patience of hope, demonstrating Christ's faithful work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's faith and His power to create working faith
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual faith (Romans 1:7)

### ELECT
- **Meaning:** "Divine choice, Christ's selection"
- **Context:** Election of God known by brethren, demonstrating Christ's chosen people
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's election and His power to choose believers
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual elect (2 Thessalonians 2:13)

### HOLY
- **Meaning:** "Divine sanctification, Christ's purity"
- **Context:** Holy Ghost bringing joy in affliction, demonstrating Christ's sanctifying presence
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's holiness and His power to sanctify suffering
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual holy (Acts 2:4)

### WAIT
- **Meaning:** "Divine anticipation, Christ's expectation"
- **Context:** Waiting for Christ's Son from heaven, demonstrating Christ's return expectation
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's waiting and His power to create hopeful anticipation
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual wait (1 Corinthians 15:20)

## THE ACTUAL VERSES (KJV)

1 Paul, and Silvanus, and Timotheus, unto the church of the Thessalonians which is in God the Father and in the Lord Jesus Christ: Grace be unto you, and peace, from God our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ.  

2 We give thanks to God always for you all, making mention of you in our prayers;  

3 Remembering without ceasing your work of faith, and labour of love, and patience of hope in our Lord Jesus Christ, in the sight of God and our Father;  

4 Knowing, brethren beloved, your election of God.  

5 For our gospel came not unto you in word only, but also in power, and in the Holy Ghost, and in much assurance; as ye know what manner of men we were among you for your sake.  

6 And ye became followers of us, and of the Lord, having received the word in much affliction, with joy of the Holy Ghost:  

7 So that ye were ensamples to all that believe in Macedonia and Achaia.  

8 For from you sounded out the word of the Lord not only in Macedonia and Achaia, but also in every place your faith to God-ward is spread abroad; so that we need not to speak any thing.  

9 For they themselves shew of us what manner of entering in we had unto you, and how ye turned to God from idols to serve the living and true God;  

10 And to wait for his Son from heaven, whom he raised from the dead, even Jesus, which delivered us from the wrath to come.  

11 And to wait for his Son from heaven, whom he raised from the dead, even Jesus, which delivered us from the wrath to come.  

12 And to wait for his Son from heaven, whom he raised from the dead, even Jesus, which delivered us from the wrath to come.  

13 And to wait for his Son from heaven, whom he raised from the dead, even Jesus, which delivered us from the wrath to come.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Remembering without ceasing your work of faith, and labour of love, and patience of hope"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any good works or efforts
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's threefold spiritual work - The work represents Christ's faith working through believers, not human achievement.

### "Wait for his Son from heaven"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any waiting or patience
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific return - The waiting represents anticipation of Christ's second coming, not general patience.
