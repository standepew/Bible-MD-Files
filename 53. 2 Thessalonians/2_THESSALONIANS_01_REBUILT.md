## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### FAITH
- **Meaning:** "Divine trust, Christ's belief"
- **Context:** Faith growing exceedingly with charity abounding, demonstrating Christ's expanding faith
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's faith and His power to grow believers' trust
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual faith (1 Thessalonians 1:3)

### JUDGMENT
- **Meaning:** "Divine justice, Christ's verdict"
- **Context:** Righteous judgment of God recompensing tribulation, demonstrating Christ's just judgment
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's judgment and His power to execute justice
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual judgment (Romans 12:12)

### REVEALED
- **Meaning:** "Divine manifestation, Christ's appearing"
- **Context:** Lord Jesus revealed from heaven with mighty angels, demonstrating Christ's glorious appearing
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's revelation and His power to be manifest with angels
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual revealed (Matthew 25:31)

### GLORIFIED
- **Meaning:** "Divine honor, Christ's exaltation"
- **Context:** Christ glorified in saints and believers, demonstrating Christ's mutual glorification
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's glorification and His power to honor believers
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual glorified (Romans 8:30)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Your faith groweth exceedingly"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any personal growth or development
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's expanding work - The growth represents Christ's power to increase faith, not human self-improvement.

### "When the Lord Jesus shall be revealed from heaven with his mighty angels"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any spiritual manifestation or angelic appearance
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific second coming - The revealed represents Christ's glorious return with angels for judgment, not general revelation.
