## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### PRAYER
- **Meaning:** "Divine communication, Christ's intercession"
- **Context:** Supplications, prayers, intercessions for all men, demonstrating Christ's intercessory prayer
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's prayer and His power to intercede for all
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual prayer (Ephesians 6:18)

### MEDIATOR
- **Meaning:** "Divine intercessor, Christ's mediation"
- **Context:** One mediator Christ Jesus giving Himself as ransom, demonstrating Christ's unique mediation
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's mediation and His power to reconcile God and men
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual mediator (Hebrews 9:15)

### HOLY
- **Meaning:** "Divine purity, Christ's sanctification"
- **Context:** Lifting holy hands without wrath, demonstrating Christ's pure worship
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's holiness and His power to purify worship
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual holy (Psalm 24:4)

### SILENCE
- **Meaning:** "Divine submission, Christ's humility"
- **Context:** Women learning in silence with subjection, demonstrating Christ's humble order
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's silence and His power to create proper order
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual silence (Genesis 3:6)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "For there is one God, and one mediator between God and men, the man Christ Jesus"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any mediation or intercession
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's exclusive mediation - The mediator represents Christ's unique role as the only way to God, not human mediators.

### "Let the woman learn in silence with all subjection"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any female silence or submission
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's created order - The silence represents Christ's design for proper authority in the church, not general female silence.
