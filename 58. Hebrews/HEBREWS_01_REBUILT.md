## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### SON
- **Meaning:** "Divine heir, Christ's supremacy"
- **Context:** God's final revelation through His Son who is heir of all things, demonstrating Christ's supreme position
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's sonship and His power to reveal God perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual son (John 1:1)

### IMAGE
- **Meaning:** "Divine likeness, Christ's representation"
- **Context:** Express image of God's person upholding all things, demonstrating Christ's perfect representation
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's image and His power to represent God completely
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual image (Colossians 1:16)

### THRONE
- **Meaning:** "Divine reign, Christ's authority"
- **Context:** Eternal throne of God with sceptre of righteousness, demonstrating Christ's everlasting authority
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's throne and His power to reign eternally
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual throne (Psalm 2:7)

### SPIRITS
- **Meaning:** "Divine messengers, Christ's servants"
- **Context:** Ministering spirits sent to serve heirs of salvation, demonstrating Christ's angelic ministry
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's spirits and His power to minister through angels
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual spirits (Deuteronomy 32:43)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "God, who at sundry times and in divers manners spake in time past unto the fathers by the prophets"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any divine communication or revelation
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific final revelation - The sundry times represent God's progressive revelation culminating in Christ, not general communication.

### "Being made so much better than the angels"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any superiority or excellence
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific divine nature - The better represents Christ's essential superiority as Son of God, not human achievement.
