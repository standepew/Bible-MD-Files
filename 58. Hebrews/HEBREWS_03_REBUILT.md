## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### APOSTLE
- **Meaning:** "Divine messenger, Christ's commission"
- **Context:** Christ as Apostle and High Priest of our profession, demonstrating Christ's divine commission
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's apostleship and His power to represent God
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual apostle (Numbers 12:7)

### HOUSE
- **Meaning:** "Divine household, Christ's people"
- **Context:** Christ as Son over His own house with believers as members, demonstrating Christ's household leadership
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's house and His power to build and maintain His people
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual house (John 1:3)

### CONFIDENCE
- **Meaning:** "Divine assurance, Christ's faith"
- **Context:** Holding fast confidence and rejoicing of hope firm to the end, demonstrating Christ's sustaining assurance
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's confidence and His power to maintain faith
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual confidence (Psalm 95:7)

### UNBELIEF
- **Meaning:** "Divine rejection, Christ's warning"
- **Context:** Evil heart of unbelief departing from living God, demonstrating Christ's warning against rejection
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's unbelief and His power to warn against rejection
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual unbelief (Exodus 17:7)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Christ as a son over his own house; whose house are we, if we hold fast the confidence and the rejoicing of the hope firm unto the end"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any church membership or religious affiliation
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific household - The house represents Christ's spiritual house of believers who maintain confidence, not physical buildings.

### "Take heed, brethren, lest there be in any of you an evil heart of unbelief, in departing from the living God"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any doubt or questioning
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific warning - The unbelief represents willful rejection of God leading to departure, not honest questioning.
