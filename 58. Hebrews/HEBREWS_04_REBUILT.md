## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### REST
- **Meaning:** "Divine cessation, Christ's salvation"
- **Context:** Promise of entering God's rest through faith, demonstrating Christ's saving rest
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's rest and His power to provide spiritual rest
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual rest (Psalm 95:11)

### LABOUR
- **Meaning:** "Divine effort, Christ's striving"
- **Context:** Labour to enter God's rest lest falling through unbelief, demonstrating Christ's striving work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's labour and His power to strive spiritually
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual labour (Romans 4:5)

### SWORD
- **Meaning:** "Divine weapon, Christ's word"
- **Context:** Word of God sharper than twoedged sword dividing soul and spirit, demonstrating Christ's powerful word
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's sword and His power to divide truthfully
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual sword (Genesis 2:2)

### THRONE
- **Meaning:** "Divine authority, Christ's reign"
- **Context:** Throne of grace for bold approach to obtain mercy, demonstrating Christ's gracious authority
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's throne and His power to reign graciously
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual throne (Psalm 95:7)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "For the word of God is quick, and powerful, and sharper than any twoedged sword"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any powerful words or persuasive speech
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific divine word - The sword represents Christ's living word that discerns hearts, not human rhetoric.

### "Let us therefore come boldly unto the throne of grace, that we may obtain mercy, and find grace to help in time of need"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any confidence or self-assurance
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific priestly access - The boldness represents confident approach through Christ's priesthood, not human confidence.
