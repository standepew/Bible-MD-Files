## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### PRIEST
- **Meaning:** "Divine mediator, Christ's intercession"
- **Context:** High priest taken from men to offer sacrifices for sins, demonstrating Christ's priestly mediation
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's priesthood and His power to mediate perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual priest (Leviticus 9:7)

### COMPASSION
- **Meaning:** "Divine mercy, Christ's sympathy"
- **Context:** High priest having compassion on the ignorant and wayward, demonstrating Christ's merciful sympathy
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's compassion and His power to sympathize with weakness
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual compassion (Exodus 28:1)

### OBEDIENCE
- **Meaning:** "Divine submission, Christ's submission"
- **Context:** Christ learned obedience through suffering, demonstrating Christ's perfect submission
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's obedience and His power to submit completely
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual obedience (Psalm 2:7)

### MEAT
- **Meaning:** "Divine nourishment, Christ's teaching"
- **Context:** Strong meat for mature believers who discern good and evil, demonstrating Christ's substantial teaching
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's meat and His power to nourish spiritually
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual meat (Psalm 110:4)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Though he were a Son, yet learned he obedience by the things which he suffered"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any learning through hardship or suffering
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific redemptive obedience - The learning represents Christ's perfect obedience through suffering, not human education.

### "But strong meat belongeth to them that are of full age, even those who by reason of use have their senses exercised to discern both good and evil"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any maturity or discernment
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific spiritual maturity - The meat represents Christ's substantial teaching for mature believers, not general wisdom.
