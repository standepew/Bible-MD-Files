- **Meaning:** "Divine incarnation, Christ's humanity"

- **Significance:** Model of Christ's body and His power to incarnate perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual body (John 1:14)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Let us draw near with a true heart in full assurance of faith, having our hearts sprinkled from an evil conscience, and our bodies washed with pure water"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any religious ritual or ceremony
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific spiritual cleansing - The sprinkling represents Christ's blood cleansing the heart, not human ritual.

### "Now the just shall live by faith: but if any man draw back, my soul shall have no pleasure in him"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any faith or belief system
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific saving faith - The faith represents Christ's justifying faith that saves, not general belief.
