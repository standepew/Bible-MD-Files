## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Looking unto Jesus the author and finisher of our faith; who for the joy that was set before him endured the cross, despising the shame"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any endurance or perseverance
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific sacrificial endurance - The cross represents Christ's sacrificial death for our joy, not human suffering.

### "For our God is a consuming fire"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any judgment or punishment
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific holy presence - The fire represents Christ's consuming holiness and purity, not destructive punishment.
