## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### TONGUE
- **Meaning:** "Divine speech, Christ's words"
- **Context:** Untamable tongue that blesses and curses, demonstrating Christ's speech power, demonstrating Christ's speaking work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's tongue and His power to speak perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual tongue (Psalm 39:1)

### WISDOM
- **Meaning:** "Divine understanding, Christ's knowledge"
- **Context:** Two kinds of wisdom - earthly vs heavenly, demonstrating Christ's understanding work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's wisdom and His power to understand perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual wisdom (Proverbs 26:3)

### POISON
- **Meaning:** "Divine corruption, Christ's danger"
- **Context:** Deadly poison in tongue that defiles, demonstrating Christ's corrupting work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's poison and His power to corrupt sinfully
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual poison (Acts 27:17)

### PEACE
- **Meaning:** "Divine harmony, Christ's reconciliation"
- **Context:** Heavenly wisdom is peaceable and produces peace, demonstrating Christ's reconciling work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's peace and His power to reconcile completely
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual peace (Psalm 34:13)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "The tongue is a fire, a world of iniquity"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any harsh speech or gossip
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific powerful speech - The fire represents Christ's powerful tongue that can destroy or bless, not human speech.

### "The wisdom that is from above is first pure, then peaceable, gentle, and easy to be intreated"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any human wisdom or intelligence
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific divine wisdom - The wisdom represents Christ's heavenly wisdom that produces peace, not human intelligence.
