- **Significance:** Model of Christ's friendship and His power to fellowship purely
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual friendship (1 John 2:15)

### SUBMIT
- **Meaning:** "Divine surrender, Christ's obedience"
- **Context:** Submit to God and resist devil, demonstrating Christ's submitting work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's submit and His power to surrender completely
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual submit (Ephesians 6:11)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Submit yourselves therefore to God. Resist the devil, and he will flee from you"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any resistance to evil or temptation
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific spiritual warfare - The resistance represents Christ's authority over evil through submission to God, not human willpower.

### "Humble yourselves in the sight of the Lord, and he shall lift you up"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any humility or modesty
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific spiritual humility - The humility represents Christ's submission to God's authority, not human self-effacement.
