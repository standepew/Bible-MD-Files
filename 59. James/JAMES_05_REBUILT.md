## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### RICHES
- **Meaning:** "Divine wealth, Christ's treasures"
- **Context:** Corrupted riches that will witness against rich men, demonstrating Christ's wealthy work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's riches and His power to treasure eternally
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual riches (Luke 12:20)

### PATIENCE
- **Meaning:** "Divine endurance, Christ's waiting"
- **Context:** Patience until Lord's coming like farmer waiting for harvest, demonstrating Christ's enduring work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's patience and His power to wait perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual patience (Matthew 6:19)

### PRAYER
- **Meaning:** "Divine communication, Christ's intercession"
- **Context:** Fervent prayer of righteous man availeth much, demonstrating Christ's communicating work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's prayer and His power to intercede effectively
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual prayer (Deuteronomy 24:14)

### SOUL
- **Meaning:** "Divine life, Christ's salvation"
- **Context:** Converting sinner saves soul from death, demonstrating Christ's saving work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's soul and His power to save completely
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual soul (Isaiah 3:15)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "The prayer of faith shall save the sick, and the Lord shall raise him up"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any healing or medical recovery
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific spiritual healing - The prayer represents Christ's healing through faith and confession, not physical medicine.

### "Confess your faults one to another, and pray one for another, that ye may be healed"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any confession or counseling
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific spiritual restoration - The confession represents Christ's healing through mutual accountability and prayer, not human psychology.
