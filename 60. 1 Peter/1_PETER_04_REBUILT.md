## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### MIND
- **Meaning:** "Divine thought, Christ's attitude"
- **Context:** Arm yourselves with same mind as Christ's suffering, demonstrating Christ's thinking work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's mind and His power to think perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual mind (Romans 6:2)

### CHARITY
- **Meaning:** "Divine love, Christ's compassion"
- **Context:** Fervent charity that covers multitude of sins, demonstrating Christ's loving work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's charity and His power to love completely
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual charity (Ephesians 4:22)

### GIFTS
- **Meaning:** "Divine abilities, Christ's endowments"
- **Context:** Spiritual gifts to be ministered as stewards of God's grace, demonstrating Christ's gifting work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's gifts and His power to endow generously
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual gifts (Acts 10:42)

### SUFFERINGS
- **Meaning:** "Divine trials, Christ's afflictions"
- **Context:** Partakers of Christ's sufferings that bring glory, demonstrating Christ's suffering work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's sufferings and His power to suffer purposefully
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual sufferings (2 Timothy 2:4)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "The end of all things is at hand: be ye therefore sober, and watch unto prayer"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any end times or prophecy
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific imminent return - The end represents Christ's return that requires spiritual readiness, not human speculation.

### "Charity shall cover the multitude of sins"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any love or forgiveness
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific sacrificial love - The charity represents Christ's covering love that forgives, not human affection.
