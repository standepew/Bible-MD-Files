## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### ELDERS
- **Meaning:** "Divine leadership, Christ's shepherding"
- **Context:** Elders to feed flock willingly as examples, demonstrating Christ's leading work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's elders and His power to lead faithfully
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual elders (John 21:15)

### HUMILITY
- **Meaning:** "Divine meekness, Christ's submission"
- **Context:** Humility clothed upon all believers, demonstrating Christ's submitting work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's humility and His power to submit completely
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual humility (Proverbs 3:34)

### DEVIL
- **Meaning:** "Divine adversary, Christ's enemy"
- **Context:** Devil as roaring lion seeking to devour, demonstrating Christ's opposing work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's devil and His power to oppose evil
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual devil (Job 1:7)

### GRACE
- **Meaning:** "Divine favor, Christ's blessing"
- **Context:** God of all grace who perfects believers after suffering, demonstrating Christ's favoring work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's grace and His power to favor eternally
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual grace (Hebrews 13:20)

1 The elders which are among you I exhort, who am also an elder, and a witness of the sufferings of Christ, and also a partaker of the glory that shall be revealed:

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Humble yourselves therefore under the mighty hand of God, that he may exalt you in due time"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any humility or meekness
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific spiritual submission - The humility represents Christ's submission to God's mighty hand for exaltation, not human modesty.

### "Be sober, be vigilant; because your adversary the devil, as a roaring lion, walketh about, seeking whom he may devour"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any danger or threat
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific spiritual warfare - The devil represents Christ's adversary that requires vigilant resistance through faith, not human fear.
