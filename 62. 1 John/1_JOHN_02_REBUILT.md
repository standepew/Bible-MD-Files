## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### ADVOCATE
- **Meaning:** "Divine intercessor, Christ's mediator"
- **Context:** Christ as advocate with Father for sins, demonstrating Christ's interceding work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's advocate and His power to intercede perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual advocate (Romans 8:34)

### COMMANDMENTS
- **Meaning:** "Divine laws, Christ's instructions"
- **Context:** Keeping commandments as proof of knowing God, demonstrating Christ's instructing work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's commandments and His power to instruct perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual commandments (1 John 4:10)

### BROTHER
- **Meaning:** "Divine family, Christ's relationship"
- **Context:** Loving vs hating brother as test of light vs darkness, demonstrating Christ's relating work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's brother and His power to relate familially
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual brother (John 13:34)

### ANOINTING
- **Meaning:** "Divine empowerment, Christ's Spirit"
- **Context:** Anointing from Holy One that teaches truth, demonstrating Christ's empowering work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's anointing and His power to empower completely
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual anointing (Deuteronomy 6:5)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Love not the world, neither the things that are in the world"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any worldly avoidance or asceticism
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific spiritual separation - The world represents Christ's opposition to fleshly lusts and pride, not human materialism.

### "If any man love the world, the love of the Father is not in him"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any balance between spiritual and earthly life
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific exclusive devotion - The love represents Christ's demand for complete devotion to Father over world, not human priorities.
