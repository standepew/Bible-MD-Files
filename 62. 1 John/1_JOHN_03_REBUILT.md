## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### SONS
- **Meaning:** "Divine children, Christ's family"
- **Context:** Believers called sons of God with future glory, demonstrating Christ's adopting work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's sons and His power to adopt completely
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual sons (John 1:12)

### RIGHTEOUSNESS
- **Meaning:** "Divine goodness, Christ's character"
- **Context:** Righteousness as evidence of knowing Christ, demonstrating Christ's character work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's righteousness and His power to be good perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual righteousness (Romans 8:29)

### SEED
- **Meaning:** "Divine life, Christ's nature"
- **Context:** God's seed remaining in believers preventing sin, demonstrating Christ's life-giving work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's seed and His power to give life completely
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual seed (Romans 6:2)

### COMPASSION
- **Meaning:** "Divine mercy, Christ's care"
- **Context:** Compassion toward needy brothers as evidence of God's love, demonstrating Christ's caring work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's compassion and His power to care mercifully
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual compassion (John 8:44)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Whosoever is born of God doth not commit sin; for his seed remaineth in him: and he cannot sin, because he is born of God"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any moral perfection or sinless living
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific spiritual nature - The seed represents Christ's divine nature that prevents habitual sin, not human perfection.

### "We know that we have passed from death unto life, because we love the brethren"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any social love or brotherly affection
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific spiritual life - The love represents Christ's life that transforms from death to life through brotherly love, not human emotion.
