## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "I am Alpha and Omega, the beginning and the ending, saith the Lord, which is, and which was, and which is to come, the Almighty"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any completeness or wholeness
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific eternal nature - The Alpha and Omega represent Christ's complete control over all of history from beginning to end, not human completeness.

### "Unto him that loved us, and washed us from our sins in his own blood"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any cleansing or forgiveness
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific sacrificial atonement - The washing represents Christ's complete cleansing from sin through His own blood, not human purification.
