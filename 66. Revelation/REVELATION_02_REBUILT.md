## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### FIRST LOVE
- **Meaning:** "Divine devotion, Christ's affection"
- **Context:** First love that Ephesus left, demonstrating Christ's loving work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's first love and His power to love completely
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual first love (Ephesians 1:7)

### TREE OF LIFE
- **Meaning:** "Divine sustenance, Christ's provision"
- **Context:** Tree of life promised to overcomers, demonstrating Christ's providing work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's tree of life and His power to provide perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual tree of life (Genesis 2:9)

### CROWN OF LIFE
- **Meaning:** "Divine reward, Christ's honor"
- **Context:** Crown of life promised to faithful martyrs, demonstrating Christ's honoring work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's crown of life and His power to honor perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual crown of life (James 1:12)

### MORNING STAR
- **Meaning:** "Divine light, Christ's illumination"
- **Context:** Morning star promised to overcomers, demonstrating Christ's illuminating work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's morning star and His power to illuminate perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual morning star (Numbers 22:12)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "Nevertheless I have somewhat against thee, because thou hast left thy first love"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any loss of enthusiasm or zeal
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific spiritual relationship - The first love represents Christ's intimate relationship with believers that can be lost through formalism, not human emotion.

### "Be thou faithful unto death, and I will give thee a crown of life"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any faithfulness or reward
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific martyrdom promise - The crown represents Christ's eternal reward for faithfulness unto death, not human achievement.
