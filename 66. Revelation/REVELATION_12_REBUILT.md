## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### WOMAN
- **Meaning:** "Divine people, Christ's church"
- **Context:** Woman clothed with sun giving birth to male child, demonstrating Christ's birthing work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's woman and His power to give birth perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual woman (Genesis 37:9)

### DRAGON
- **Meaning:** "Divine opposition, Christ's enemy"
- **Context:** Great red dragon with seven heads and ten horns, demonstrating Christ's opposing work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's dragon and His power to oppose completely
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual dragon (Isaiah 27:1)

### MICHAEL
- **Meaning:** "Divine warrior, Christ's protector"
- **Context:** Michael fighting dragon in heaven, demonstrating Christ's protecting work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's Michael and His power to protect perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual Michael (Daniel 12:1)

### WILDERNESS
- **Meaning:** "Divine refuge, Christ's protection"
- **Context:** Woman fleeing to wilderness for protection, demonstrating Christ's protecting work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's wilderness and His power to protect perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual wilderness (Jude 1:9)

1 And there appeared a great wonder in heaven; a woman clothed with the sun, and the moon under her feet, and upon her head a crown of twelve stars:  

2 And she being with child cried, travailing in birth, and pained to be delivered.  

3 And there appeared another wonder in heaven; and behold a great red dragon, having seven heads and ten horns, and seven crowns upon his heads.

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "And there appeared a great wonder in heaven; a woman clothed with the sun, and the moon under her feet, and upon her head a crown of twelve stars"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any celestial signs or astrology
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific church symbolism - The woman represents Christ's church giving birth to Him, with cosmic imagery showing divine glory and authority, not astronomical phenomena.

### "And they overcame him by the blood of the Lamb, and by the word of their testimony; and they loved not their lives unto the death"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any victory or courage
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific spiritual victory - The overcoming represents Christ's complete victory through His sacrificial blood and believers' faithful testimony, not human bravery.
