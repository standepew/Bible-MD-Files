- **Meaning:** "Divine opposition, Christ's enemy"
- **Context:** Beast from sea with seven heads and ten horns, demonstrating Christ's opposing work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's beast and His power to oppose completely

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "And all that dwell upon the earth shall worship him, whose names are not written in the book of life of the Lamb slain from the foundation of the world"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any worship or following
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific spiritual deception - The worship represents Christ's complete deception of those not saved, not human admiration.

### "Here is wisdom. Let him that hath understanding count the number of the beast: for it is the number of a man; and his number is Six hundred threescore and six"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any calculation or numerology
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific symbolic number - The 666 represents Christ's symbolic number of human imperfection and opposition to divine perfection, not human mathematics.
