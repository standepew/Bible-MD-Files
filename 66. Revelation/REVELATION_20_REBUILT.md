- **Significance:** Model of Christ's thousand years and His power to reign perfectly

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "And they lived and reigned with Christ a thousand years"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any long period or reign
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific millennial kingdom - The thousand years represent Christ's complete reign with resurrected martyrs, not human government.

### "And whosoever was not found written in the book of life was cast into the lake of fire"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any punishment or rejection
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific eternal judgment - The lake of fire represents Christ's complete eternal punishment for those not saved, not human suffering.
