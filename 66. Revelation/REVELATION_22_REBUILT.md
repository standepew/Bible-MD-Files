## KEY TERMS WITH CONTEXTUAL DEFINITIONS

### RIVER OF LIFE
- **Meaning:** "Divine refreshment, Christ's eternal life"
- **Context:** Pure river of life proceeding from God's throne, demonstrating Christ's life-giving work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's river of life and His power to give life perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual river of life (Genesis 2:10)

### TREE OF LIFE
- **Meaning:** "Divine sustenance, Christ's provision"
- **Context:** Tree of life with twelve fruits for healing nations, demonstrating Christ's providing work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's tree of life and His power to provide perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual tree of life (Ezekiel 47:12)

### ALPHA OMEGA
- **Meaning:** "Divine completeness, Christ's eternality"
- **Context:** Christ as Alpha and Omega beginning and end, demonstrating Christ's eternal work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's Alpha Omega and His power to complete perfectly
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual Alpha Omega (Revelation 7:3)

### WATER OF LIFE
- **Meaning:** "Divine salvation, Christ's redemption"
- **Context:** Free invitation to take water of life, demonstrating Christ's redeeming work
- **Significance:** Model of Christ's water of life and His power to save freely
* **New Testament parallel:** Spiritual water of life (1 John 3:2)

## COMMONLY MISQUOTED PASSAGES

### "And let him that is athirst come. And whosoever will, let him take the water of life freely"
**Common mispretation:** This is about any refreshment or invitation
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific salvation invitation - The water of life represents Christ's complete free salvation through faith, not human generosity.

### "Surely I come quickly. Amen. Even so, come, Lord Jesus"
**Common misinterpretation:* This is about any return or arrival
**Biblical context:** This is about Christ's specific second coming - The quick coming represents Christ's imminent return in glory, not human travel.
