## Category 1: Messianic Prophecies — First Coming (P001–P031)
*All 31 confirmed as historically fulfilled in the life, death, and resurrection of Jesus Christ*

### The Nativity Cluster (P001–P004)
These four prophecies governed the circumstances of the Messiah's birth with mathematical precision:

**P001 — The Virgin Birth** | [[ISAIAH_07_REBUILT|Isa 7:14]] → [[MATTHEW_01_REBUILT|Matt 1:23]]
> *"Behold, a virgin shall conceive and bear a Son, and shall call His name Immanuel."* — Isaiah 7:14
The Hebrew *almah* (young woman / virgin) was rendered *parthenos* (virgin) in the LXX, which Matthew quotes directly. The sign was not merely supernatural birth but *Immanuel* — God with us, the incarnation itself.

**P002 — Bethlehem** | [[MICAH_05_REBUILT|Mic 5:2]] → [[MATTHEW_02_REBUILT|Matt 2:1–6]]
> *"But you, Bethlehem Ephrathah, though you are little among the thousands of Judah, yet out of you shall come forth to Me the One to be Ruler in Israel, whose goings forth are from of old, from everlasting."*
The Messiah's birth location specified 700 years in advance. Note: *"from everlasting"* asserts his pre-existence and eternal nature.

**P003 — Flight to Egypt** | [[HOSEA_11_REBUILT|Hos 11:1]] → [[MATTHEW_02_REBUILT|Matt 2:14–15]]
> *"Out of Egypt I called My Son."* Hosea speaks of Israel's Exodus but Matthew sees Christ recapitulating Israel's history — he is the true Israel.

**P004 — The Massacre** | [[JEREMIAH_31_REBUILT|Jer 31:15]] → [[MATTHEW_02_REBUILT|Matt 2:17–18]]
> *"A voice was heard in Ramah, lamentation and bitter weeping, Rachel weeping for her children."*

### The Ministry Cluster (P005–P011)
**P005 — The Forerunner** | [[MALACHI_03_REBUILT|Mal 3:1]]; [[MALACHI_04_REBUILT|Mal 4:5]]; [[ISAIAH_40_REBUILT|Isa 40:3]] → [[MATTHEW_11_REBUILT|Matt 11:10–14]]; [[MARK_01_REBUILT|Mark 1:2–3]]
John the Baptist fulfilled all three forerunner prophecies. Jesus explicitly identified him as Elijah in spirit and function (Matt 17:12–13). Note: Malachi 4:5–6 has a *second* fulfillment still ahead — see **P005b** in the Two Witnesses section (Elijah literally appears again in Rev 11).

**P009 — Triumphal Entry** | [[ZECHARIAH_09_REBUILT|Zech 9:9]] → [[MATTHEW_21_REBUILT|Matt 21:1–9]]; [[JOHN_12_REBUILT|John 12:15]]
> *"Behold, your King is coming to you, lowly and riding on a donkey, a colt, the foal of a donkey."*
Jesus deliberately fulfilled this on Palm Sunday (Nisan 10 — the day Passover lambs were selected). The timing was not coincidental: the crowd brought him in on exactly the day Israel was to select the Passover lamb.

### The Passion Cluster (P012–P028)
*These 17 prophecies were all fulfilled within a 24-hour period:*

**P013 — 30 Pieces of Silver** | [[ZECHARIAH_11_REBUILT|Zech 11:12–13]] → [[MATTHEW_26_REBUILT|Matt 26:15]]; [[MATTHEW_27_REBUILT|Matt 27:6–10]]
The exact amount *and* the exact fate of the money (thrown into the temple, used for a potter's field) were prophesied 500 years earlier.

**P020 — Hands and Feet Pierced** | [[PSALM_022_REBUILT|Psa 22:16]]; [[ZECHARIAH_12_REBUILT|Zech 12:10]] → [[JOHN_20_REBUILT|John 20:25]]; [[JOHN_19_REBUILT|John 19:37]]
Psalm 22 describes crucifixion in perfect detail 1,000 years before crucifixion was invented as a method of execution.

**P023 — Casting Lots** | [[PSALM_022_REBUILT|Psa 22:18]] → [[JOHN_19_REBUILT|John 19:24]]
John quotes this explicitly, noting the Roman soldiers fulfilled it without knowing they were fulfilling Scripture.

**P025 — No Bones Broken** | [[PSALM_034_REBUILT|Psa 34:20]]; [[EXO_12_REBUILT|Exo 12:46]] → [[JOHN_19_REBUILT|John 19:33–36]]
This links Christ explicitly to the Passover Lamb (whose bones could not be broken — Exo 12:46). Both a prophetic fulfillment *and* a typological one simultaneously.

**P029 — Resurrection** | [[PSALM_016_REBUILT|Psa 16:10]] → [[ACTS_02_REBUILT|Acts 2:31]]; [[ACTS_13_REBUILT|Acts 13:35]]

## Category 2: Israel National Prophecies (P032–P042)

### The Exile and Return (P032–P034) — All Fulfilled
**P034 — Cyrus Named 150 Years Early** | [[ISAIAH_44_REBUILT|Isa 44:28–45:1]]
Isaiah called Cyrus by name approximately 150 years before his birth, specifying that he would issue a decree to rebuild Jerusalem. This remains one of the most specific predictive prophecies in Scripture.

### The Destruction of Jerusalem (P035–P036)
**P035 — Temple Destroyed** | [[DANIEL_09_REBUILT|Dan 9:26]]; [[MATTHEW_24_REBUILT|Matt 24:2]] → Fulfilled 70 AD
Jesus prophesied that not one stone would remain on another. Fulfilled precisely when Titus burned the Temple — the fire melted the gold, which ran between the stones, causing the Romans to tear every stone apart to recover it.

### The Regathering (P038–P041) — Partially Fulfilled
**P039 — Nation Born in a Day** | [[ISAIAH_66_REBUILT|Isa 66:8]] → May 14, 1948
> *"Who has heard such a thing? Who has seen such things? Shall the earth be made to give birth in one day? Or shall a nation be born at once? For as soon as Zion was in labor, she gave birth to her children."*
Israel declared independence on May 14, 1948 — the United States recognized it the same day. A nation restored after 1,878 years of dispersion, in a single day.

**P040 — Valley of Dry Bones** | [[EZEKIEL_37_REBUILT|Ezek 37:1–14]] → 1948 (partial)
The vision of bones coming together, flesh forming, and breath entering — stages the national restoration of Israel. 1948 is the bones coming together and the flesh forming. The *breath* (the Spirit of God) = the future national conversion at Christ's return (P042).

**P041 — Jerusalem Reunified** | [[LUKE_21_REBUILT|Luke 21:24]]; [[ZECHARIAH_08_REBUILT|Zech 8:4–8]] → June 7, 1967 (partial)
> *"Jerusalem will be trampled by Gentiles until the times of the Gentiles are fulfilled."* — Luke 21:24
The Six-Day War, June 1967: Israel captured Jerusalem for the first time since 586 BC. The *Times of the Gentiles* appear to be in their final phase.

## Category 3: Time of the End — The Great Distress / Day of the LORD (P043–P052)

> These prophecies have not yet been fulfilled. They concern Daniel's 70th Week — the final 7 years of the 490 years decreed upon Israel (Dan 9:24–27) — and include the "Time of Jacob's Trouble" (Jer 30:7), the "1,260 Days" (Rev 11:3; 12:6), and the "42 Months" (Rev 11:2; 13:5). The text defines the period by its own markers, not by systematic-theological labels.

### The Temple and the Antichrist (P043–P048)
**P048 — The Defiled Sanctuary (miqdash)** | [[DANIEL_09_REBUILT|Dan 9:27]]; [[DANIEL_08_REBUILT|Dan 8:13]]; [[MATTHEW_24_REBUILT|Matt 24:15]]; [[2_THESSALONIANS_02_REBUILT|2 Thess 2:4]]
For the Abomination of Desolation (P043) to occur, a functioning place of sacrifice (miqdash — Dan 8:13) must exist in Jerusalem. The text requires a consecrated location where a sacrifice can be stopped (Dan 9:27: "he shall cause the sacrifice and the offering to cease"), not a specific architectural grade of building. As of 2026, the Temple Mount remains occupied by the Dome of the Rock. NOTE: This sanctuary is distinct from (1) the Ezekiel 40–48 structure (millennial, post-Day of the LORD) and (2) the New Jerusalem (Rev 21:22 — "I saw no temple therein"). These are three separate data points on the scriptural timeline.

**P043 — Abomination of Desolation** | [[DANIEL_09_REBUILT|Dan 9:27]]; [[DANIEL_11_REBUILT|Dan 11:31]]; [[MATTHEW_24_REBUILT|Matt 24:15]] | Status: 🔀 DUAL
- *Type/Near fulfillment:* Antiochus IV Epiphanes, 168 BC — sacrificed a pig on the altar, erected a statue of Zeus
- *Antitype/Far fulfillment:* The Man of Sin will do this in the defiled sanctuary at the midpoint of the 70th Week (2 Thess 2:4; Dan 9:27 — "in the midst of the week")
Jesus (speaking after 168 BC) said this was *still future* — confirming a second, greater fulfillment is coming.

**P044 — The Man of Sin** | [[2_THESSALONIANS_02_REBUILT|2 Thess 2:3–4]]; [[DANIEL_07_REBUILT|Dan 7:25]]; [[REVELATION_13_REBUILT|Rev 13:5–8]]
> *"The man of sin...who opposes and exalts himself above all that is called God or that is worshiped, so that he sits as God in the temple of God, showing himself that he is God."*

**P045 — The Mark of the Beast** | [[REVELATION_13_REBUILT|Rev 13:16–17]]
> *"He causes all, both small and great, rich and poor, free and slave, to receive a mark on their right hand or on their forehead, and that no one may buy or sell except one who has the mark."*
**Current Events Context:** The convergence of CBDC (Central Bank Digital Currency), digital ID systems, biometric technology, and AI-driven surveillance constitutes *infrastructure* for the mark system — but the mark itself is a distinct future event tied to worship of the Antichrist (Rev 14:9–11). See [[NEWS_PROPHECY_MAP]] for current technology tracking.

### The Two Witnesses (P046)
**P046 — Two Witnesses** | [[REVELATION_11_REBUILT|Rev 11:3–12]]; [[ZECHARIAH_04_REBUILT|Zech 4:3–14]]; [[MALACHI_04_REBUILT|Mal 4:5]]
Two individuals will prophesy in Jerusalem for 1,260 days (Rev 11:3 — the first half of the 70th Week), performing signs like Moses and Elijah. They are killed, their bodies lie in the streets for 3.5 days, and they are resurrected before the world. Their identity is debated but the Elijah-Moses typology is deliberate.

### The Gog-Magog War (P051)
**P051** | [[EZEKIEL_38_REBUILT|Ezek 38]]; [[EZEKIEL_39_REBUILT|Ezek 39]]
A coalition identified as Rosh (Russia), Persia (Iran), Ethiopia (Cush), Libya (Put), Gomer (Turkey/Germany), and Togarmah (Turkey) invades Israel *"in the latter years"* when Israel is *"living securely."*
**Current Events Context:** The Russia-Iran-Turkey alliance (formed 2015–present), all described in this coalition, is unprecedented in history and corresponds precisely to what Ezekiel describes. See [[NEWS_PROPHECY_MAP]] for current developments.

## Category 4: Second Coming Prophecies (P053–P056)

**P053 — The Second Coming on Clouds** | [[DANIEL_07_REBUILT|Dan 7:13]]; [[MATTHEW_24_REBUILT|Matt 24:30]]; [[REVELATION_01_REBUILT|Rev 1:7]]
> *"Behold, He is coming with clouds, and every eye will see Him, even they who pierced Him. And all the tribes of the earth will mourn because of Him."*

**P054 — Every Eye Will See** | [[REVELATION_01_REBUILT|Rev 1:7]]; [[ZECHARIAH_12_REBUILT|Zech 12:10]]
A global simultaneous visual event — possible via satellite broadcast (modern technology makes this physically comprehensible for the first time in history; in John's day it seemed impossible).

**P055 — Mount of Olives Splits** | [[ZECHARIAH_14_REBUILT|Zech 14:4]]
> *"His feet will stand on the Mount of Olives, which faces Jerusalem on the east. And the Mount of Olives shall be split in two...making a very large valley."*
Jesus ascended from the Mount of Olives (Acts 1:12). The angels said he would return the same way (Acts 1:11). The geological fault line runs directly under the Mount of Olives — the mechanism exists.

## Category 5: Millennial and Eternal State Prophecies (P057–P060)

**P058 — The Millennial Kingdom** | [[REVELATION_20_REBUILT|Rev 20:4–6]]; [[ISAIAH_65_REBUILT|Isa 65:20–25]]; [[ZECHARIAH_14_REBUILT|Zech 14:9]]
A literal 1,000-year reign of Christ on earth, centered in Jerusalem. Key features:
- Extended lifespans (Isa 65:20 — those who die at 100 are considered accursed youths)
- Full knowledge of God fills the earth (Isa 11:9)
- Creation restored — wolves with lambs (Isa 11:6)
- Resurrected saints reign with Christ (Rev 20:4)
- Satan bound — no temptation from that source (Rev 20:2)

**P059–P060 — New Creation** | [[REVELATION_21_REBUILT|Rev 21–22]]; [[ISAIAH_65_REBUILT|Isa 65:17]]
The eternal state: no sea (symbolic of chaos/separation), no sun needed (Christ is the light), no temple (God dwells with men directly), no death, no tears, no pain. The New Jerusalem = 1,400 miles on each side — a perfect cube echoing the Most Holy Place of the Tabernacle.
