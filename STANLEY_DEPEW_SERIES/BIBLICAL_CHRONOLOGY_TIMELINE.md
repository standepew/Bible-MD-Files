## II. The Biological Seal: The 42 Generations

**Matthew 1:17** — "So all the generations from Abraham to David are **fourteen generations**; and from David until the carrying away into Babylon are **fourteen generations**; and from the carrying away into Babylon unto Christ are **fourteen generations**."

- 14 + 14 + 14 = **42 Generations**
- This number **42** mirrors the **42 months** (3.5 years) of Daniel 9:27 and Revelation 13:5

## III. The Anchor: The 15th Year of Tiberius

**Luke 3:1** — The historical anchor connecting the AM count to recorded history.

**Luke 3:27 AD** — "Now in the fifteenth year of the reign of Tiberius Caesar..."
